A tube-rotator module with front and rear bearing hoops, a central cylindrical carrier, and a small bracket fixed to the rotating carrier. The supports should feel coaxial and balanced around the rotating body, with the carrier clearly captured between them. Make it feel real with machined bearing rings, retainer caps, frame ribs, and believable wall thickness in both the rotating carrier and fixed supports. Keep every part mechanically grounded with no floating members, and make sure the articulation paths stay clear so nothing clips through covers, stops, housings, rails, or neighboring links.
