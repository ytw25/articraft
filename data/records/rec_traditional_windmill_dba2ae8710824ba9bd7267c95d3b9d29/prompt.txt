Design Traditional windmill as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a tower with rotor hub, blade lattice, and cap/nacelle support. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Rotor/cap stages should be shaft-supported with clear bearing seats. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
