A desktop ring light on a weighted base with a telescoping mast. The circular light head is held by a small yoke at the top of the mast, and a clamp-style phone holder sits at the center of the ring. The head rotates on a horizontal revolute tilt axis at the yoke, the mast slides prismatically along the vertical axis, and the phone holder jaws move as opposed short prismatic sliders within the clamp frame.
