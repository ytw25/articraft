A heavy-duty office laptop riser with a thick base slab, a large top tray with corner pads, and two wide triangular side link assemblies that create a parallel-lift structure. The tray is connected to the base by paired upper and lower arms on each side, with the pivots exposed as round hinge bosses near the front and rear corners. Each arm rotates on a revolute joint at the base and another revolute joint at the tray, producing a parallel-link motion that raises and lowers the tray.
