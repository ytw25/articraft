A combination discus padlock with a round body and three number dials on the face. The shielded shackle is captive in the body at one side, and the number wheels are mounted on parallel axles below the exposed shackle opening. The shackle rotates upward on its captive pivot axis, and the three number dials rotate independently on their own local axes. Keep the three number dials clipped on their face shafts so the wheels rotate without floating off the lock body.
