A desktop computer motherboard with a large CPU socket, long expansion slots, and a bank of memory slots. The rigid PCB carries heatsink blocks and connector walls, while the socket lever, memory-slot latches, and expansion-slot retention clip are mounted directly to their respective connectors. The CPU socket lever rotates on a small revolute pivot at the socket edge, each memory-slot latch rotates on its own tiny hinge at the slot end, and the expansion-slot clip rotates on a short pivot at the slot tail.
