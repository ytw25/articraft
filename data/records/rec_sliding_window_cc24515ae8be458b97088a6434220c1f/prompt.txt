Model Sliding window as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a framed window with one sliding sash in channel guides, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Sash motion should stay in channels with believable overlaps and stops. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
