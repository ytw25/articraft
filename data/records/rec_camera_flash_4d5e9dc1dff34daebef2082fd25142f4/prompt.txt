A hot-shoe flash with a boxy lamp head, a short articulated neck, and a small rear screen, plus a rear command wheel below the screen. The head and neck stay visibly separate from the battery body, and the command wheel is a real raised control instead of a printed circle. The neck swivels on a vertical revolute joint above the body, the flash head tilts on a horizontal revolute joint at the top of the neck, and the command wheel rotates continuously on its center axis. Mirrorless-camera accessory scale.
