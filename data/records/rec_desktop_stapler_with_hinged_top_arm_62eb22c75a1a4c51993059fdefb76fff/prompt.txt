A premium desktop stapler with a broad base, a rear-hinged staple magazine, a tapered top arm mounted on a separate hinge at the back of the magazine, and a rear spring follower inside the magazine. The magazine rotates on a rear horizontal revolute joint above the base, the top arm rotates on its own rear revolute joint at the magazine spine, the follower block slides prismatically along the magazine channel, and a small front anvil rotates on a pivot in the base. Standard office-desk scale.
