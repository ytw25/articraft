A compact kitchen juicer with a boxy motor base, a clear domed lid, and a cylindrical feed pusher above the cutting basket. A rocker switch is mounted on the lower front face, and the short juice spout carries a hinged cap at its tip. The feed pusher slides prismatically along the chute axis, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the rocker switch rotates on a short pivot, the spout cap rotates on a small hinge, and the basket rotates continuously about the vertical drive axis.
