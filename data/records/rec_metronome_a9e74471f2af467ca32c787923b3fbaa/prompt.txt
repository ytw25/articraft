A compact travel metronome with a short rectangular housing and a fold-flat hinged foot stand at the base. A thin pendulum rod with a disc-shaped weight swings from a top interior pivot; a cylindrical winding key sits flush in the back face. The stand rotates on a revolute hinge at the housing bottom; the pendulum swings on a revolute joint at the top shaft; the weight rides prismatically on the rod; the key turns continuously.
