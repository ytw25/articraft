An all-in-one style display with a tall rear support arm, a rotating screen panel, and a flip-down cable cover on the back of the stand. The screen is carried by the upper stand head over a low base, and a small control knob is mounted along the lower side edge. The display rotates on a horizontal tilt hinge at the stand head, the screen rotates on a vertical-axis portrait joint, the cable cover rotates on a small rear hinge on the stand arm, and the control knob rotates on its own short shaft.
