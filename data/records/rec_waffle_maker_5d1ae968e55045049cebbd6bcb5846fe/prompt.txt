A round Belgian waffle maker with a circular lower housing, a domed top plate, and a compact front control cluster with three distinct program buttons and one thermostat knob. The top half rotates open on a rear revolute hinge, the thermostat knob rotates continuously about its central axis, and all three program buttons are separate prismatic push buttons mounted in the front cluster. Realistic kitchen-counter scale with a sturdy hinge bracket, front handle, and vented side walls.
