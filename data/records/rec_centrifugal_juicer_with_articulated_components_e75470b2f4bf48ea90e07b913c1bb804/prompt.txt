A countertop centrifugal juicer with a rounded base, a clear upper chamber lid, and a tall cylindrical feed pusher moving through the top chute. A removable pulp bin slides out from the rear of the base, and a speed dial sits on one side of the front face. The pusher slides prismatically along the chute axis, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the pulp bin slides prismatically out from the rear housing, the speed dial rotates on its own short shaft, and the basket rotates continuously about the vertical drive axis.
