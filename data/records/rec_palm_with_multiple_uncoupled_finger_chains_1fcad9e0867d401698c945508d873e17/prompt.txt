A radial gripper palm with four equal finger chains arranged around a square central hub. Each finger is a two-link rigid chain attached at one side of the palm hub, with no thumb and no mechanical coupling between fingers. Every finger root rotates on its own base pivot at the hub, and the second joint of each chain rotates on its own local hinge axis between the two links. Keep each two-link finger chain clipped together at its hinge pivot so the radial fingers stay captured while bending.
