A work-truck tailgate with a lower main gate and a top-hinged mesh extension panel. The main panel is mounted on lower corner hinges between the bed sides, and the lightweight upper extension is hinged along the top edge of the main gate. The main gate rotates on a horizontal lower hinge axis, and the mesh extension rotates on its own horizontal top-edge hinge axis. Keep the top-hinged mesh extension clipped to the upper edge of the main gate so it stays attached while lifting.
