Model Wheelchair as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a chair frame with large drive wheels, front casters, and footrests, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Drive wheels, caster swivel, and frame joints should be mechanically explicit. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
