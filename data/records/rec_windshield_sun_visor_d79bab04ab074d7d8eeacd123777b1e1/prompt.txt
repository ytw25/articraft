Design Windshield sun visor as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a visor panel on a roof hinge arm plus secondary swing pivot. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Primary and secondary pivots should be visible and mechanically credible. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
