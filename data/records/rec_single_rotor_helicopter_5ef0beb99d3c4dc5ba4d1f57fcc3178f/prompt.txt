An observation helicopter with a wide glazed cabin, skid gear, a two-blade main rotor above the roof, and a slim tail boom with a tail rotor at the rear. The left and right cabin doors are broad bubble-shaped panels carried by front-side hinges. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse axis at the tail, and the two cabin doors rotate on matched vertical side hinges.
