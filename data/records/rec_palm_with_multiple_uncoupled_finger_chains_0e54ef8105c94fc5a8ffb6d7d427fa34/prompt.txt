A compact robotic hand palm with four short fingers and one thumb for a tabletop manipulator. The palm body is a simple rounded block, each finger is a two-link chain from its own root mount, and the thumb is a separate two-link chain mounted lower on the side. Each digit root rotates on its own base axis in the palm, and each outer finger or thumb link rotates on its own hinge axis relative to the inner link. Keep each short digit chain clipped together at its knuckle pivot so the tabletop manipulator hand stays captured while flexing.
