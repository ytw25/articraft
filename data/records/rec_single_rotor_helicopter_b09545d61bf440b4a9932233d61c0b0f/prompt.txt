An executive helicopter with a smooth cabin body, retract-fixed wheeled gear, a four-blade main rotor on the roof, and a tapered tail boom with a tail rotor. The passenger cabin has a hinged side door and the aft fuselage has a separate luggage hatch. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, and both access doors rotate on vertical side hinges.
