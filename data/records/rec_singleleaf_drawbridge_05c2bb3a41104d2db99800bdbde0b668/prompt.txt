Model Singleleaf drawbridge as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a fixed support frame and one rotating bridge leaf on side bearings, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Bridge leaf rotation should be supported by heavy side pivots/trunnions. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
