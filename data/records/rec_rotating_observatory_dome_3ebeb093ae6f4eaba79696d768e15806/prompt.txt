Design Rotating observatory dome as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a dome shell seated on a ring track with a slit aperture subsystem. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Dome rotation and slit mechanics should ride on supported tracks/bearings. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
