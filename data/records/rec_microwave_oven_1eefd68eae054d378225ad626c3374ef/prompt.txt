A countertop microwave oven with a side-hinged front door and two rotary dials. A rectangular housing encloses the cavity and supports the door on one side, while a glass turntable sits on a support ring inside the chamber. The door rotates on a vertical revolute hinge axis, the two control dials rotate about front-to-back axes in the control panel, and the turntable rotates on a vertical axis inside the cavity.
