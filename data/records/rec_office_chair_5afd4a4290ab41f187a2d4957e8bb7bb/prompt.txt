An executive office chair with padded arms, a high back, and flip-up armrests. The five-star wheeled base carries the seat on a central gas lift, the backrest mounts behind the seat, and each armrest is hinged at its rear bracket. The caster wheels rotate on their axles, the caster forks swivel on vertical pivots, the seat swivels about the gas column, the gas column slides vertically, the backrest reclines on a horizontal pivot, and the two armrests rotate upward on their hinge axes. Keep the gas column clipped into both the seat mechanism and the star base so the chair stays stacked on the pedestal during swivel and height adjustment.
