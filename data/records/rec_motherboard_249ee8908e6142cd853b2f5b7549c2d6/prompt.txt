A workstation motherboard with a long board shape, multiple PCIe slots, and a hinged CPU socket load plate. The board carries the socket area near the top, banks of memory slots at the side, and several expansion slots aligned along the lower half. The CPU socket load plate rotates on its rear hinge axis, the socket lever rotates on its own local pivot, the DIMM latches rotate on small hinge axes, and the PCIe slot latches rotate on their slot-end pivots. Keep the CPU socket load plate clipped to its hinge at the socket body so the plate stays captured during opening.
