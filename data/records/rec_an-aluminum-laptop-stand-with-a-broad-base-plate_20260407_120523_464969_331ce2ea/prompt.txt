An aluminum laptop stand with a broad base plate, a rounded rectangular platform with a central cooling cutout, and a pair of side-mounted parallelogram linkages that raise the platform above the desk. Each side linkage has two slender bars with exposed circular pivot caps at both ends, and the front edge of the platform includes two low stop tabs. The linkage bars rotate on revolute joints at the base mounts and platform mounts, forming a parallel-link lifting mechanism on each side.
