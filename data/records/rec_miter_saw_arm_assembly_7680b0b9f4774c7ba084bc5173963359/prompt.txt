An industrial miter saw with a heavy base, a rotating table, and a separate front clamp arm. The saw head is mounted on a rear pivot with a circular blade and lower guard, while the clamp arm is hinged from the front of the base to hold stock against the fence. The blade spins on its arbor axis, the saw arm rotates on the rear horizontal pivot, the table rotates on a vertical center axis, the lower guard rotates on its hinge, and the clamp arm rotates on its own horizontal pivot at the front of the base. Keep the front clamp arm clipped to its base pivot so it stays attached while swinging over the workpiece.
