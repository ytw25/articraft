A roof-mount satellite dish with a shallow reflector on a short pedestal yoke. The dish and feed arm are fixed to a rear frame that sits in side trunnions above a rotating pedestal. The pedestal rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, and the dish frame rotates about a horizontal elevation axis through the side trunnions. Keep the rear dish frame clipped between the elevation side pivots above the pedestal so the reflector stays supported while tilting.
