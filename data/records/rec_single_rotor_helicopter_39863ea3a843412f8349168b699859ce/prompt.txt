A fire utility helicopter with a robust fuselage, skid landing gear, a five-blade main rotor, and a tail boom carrying a tail rotor near the tip. The engine bay has one service door on each side behind the mast, and the cabin has a crew access door on one side. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the two service doors rotate on side hinges, and the crew door rotates on a vertical side hinge.
