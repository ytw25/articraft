A 24-inch office desktop monitor with a rectangular display shell, a slim central neck, and a flat pedestal base, plus a rear cable-cover door on the stand spine. the lower bezel leaves room for visible front-panel controls instead of a blank strip, and the cable door is a real supported flap rather than a seam line. The screen housing tilts on a horizontal revolute hinge at the top of the neck and the stand swivels continuously about a vertical axis above the base, and the cable-cover door rotates on a small hinge along one vertical edge. Standard office-desk scale.
