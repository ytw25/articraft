A retro-style pop-up toaster with a polished body, a broad side lever, and a large round shade selector near the lower front. The housing has two top slots and a removable crumb tray beneath the slot area, with a small cancel button above the selector. The bread carriage moves prismatically up and down inside the main body, the side lever moves vertically with the carriage on a parallel prismatic guide, the shade selector rotates on its front axis, the cancel button moves on a short prismatic plunger, and the crumb tray slides prismatically from the lower shell.
