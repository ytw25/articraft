A service-body access hatch with a rear-hinged door and two quarter-turn latches on the front face. The rigid perimeter frame is fixed to the body opening, and the flush outer panel overlaps the frame on a top rear hinge line. The panel rotates upward on a horizontal rear hinge axis, and each quarter-turn latch rotates on its own local axis near the lower corners. Keep the service hatch panel clipped to the rear hinge line so the lid stays seated on the frame during opening.
