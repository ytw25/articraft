A slim creator laptop with a lift-hinge display that raises the rear of the base slightly when opened. The display lid is linked to the rear hinge mechanism so its back edge acts against a small rear support lip on the lower chassis. The lid rotates on the rear hinge axis, the keycaps move as short vertical prismatic plungers, and the rear support lip rotates slightly on its hinge linkage as the screen opens. Keep the lift-hinge support lip clipped to its linkage at the rear of the base so the support stays attached as the lid opens.
