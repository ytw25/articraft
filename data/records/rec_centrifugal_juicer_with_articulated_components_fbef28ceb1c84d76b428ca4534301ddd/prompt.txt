A compact countertop juicer with a rounded base, a hinged top lid, and a vertical feed pusher above the juicing basket. A short anti-drip spout projects from the front of the chamber, and a small speed lever sits on the side of the base. The feed pusher slides prismatically in the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the anti-drip spout rotates on a small front pivot, the speed lever rotates on a short side pivot, and the juicing basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
