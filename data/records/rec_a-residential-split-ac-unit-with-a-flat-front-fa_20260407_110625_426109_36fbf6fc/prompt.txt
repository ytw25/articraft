A residential split AC unit with a flat front fascia, two push buttons at the right end, and a lower swing flap with inner air-directing fins. The main shell is fixed, and the outlet assembly is built from one long front flap and several slim internal fins. The two buttons move on short prismatic plungers into the housing, the front flap rotates on a horizontal hinge at the outlet edge, and the internal fins rotate independently on their own pivots.
