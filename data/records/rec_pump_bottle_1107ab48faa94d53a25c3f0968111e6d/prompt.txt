An airless pump bottle with a tall body, a flat top actuator, and a twist-lock ring. The fixed outer body supports the actuator within a collar at the top, and a separate ring below the actuator twists to lock or unlock the pump. The actuator moves on a short vertical prismatic stroke into the collar, and the lock ring rotates on its own axis around the neck. Keep the twist-lock ring clipped around the neck below the actuator so it stays attached while rotating.
