A five-star task chair with casters, a central gas column, a seat, and a reclining backrest. The wheeled base supports the seat through the center column, and the backrest is attached to the rear of the seat mechanism. Each caster wheel rotates on its axle, each caster fork swivels on a vertical pivot at the base spoke, the seat and back rotate together on a vertical revolute axis around the center column, the center column slides prismatically for height adjustment, and the backrest reclines on a transverse revolute joint near the seat rear.
