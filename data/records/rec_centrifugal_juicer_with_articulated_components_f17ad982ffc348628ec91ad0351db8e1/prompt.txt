A stainless steel juicer with a deep motor base, a clear hinged lid, and a cylindrical feed pusher traveling through the center chute. One side of the upper body has a hinged service door, and a pulse button sits low on the front panel. The pusher slides prismatically along the chute axis, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the service door rotates on a vertical side hinge, the pulse button moves on a short prismatic plunger, and the grater basket rotates continuously about the vertical drive axis.
