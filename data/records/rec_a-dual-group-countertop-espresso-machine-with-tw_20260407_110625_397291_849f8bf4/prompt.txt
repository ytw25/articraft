A dual-group countertop espresso machine with two front group heads, one steam wand at each outer side, and a hinged service lid over the water tank area. Two portafilters mount below the group heads, and a long drip tray spans the base below a raised cup grate. Each portafilter rotates on its own group-head axis, each steam wand rotates on its side support joint, the service lid rotates on a top hinge, and the long drip tray slides prismatically out from the front.
