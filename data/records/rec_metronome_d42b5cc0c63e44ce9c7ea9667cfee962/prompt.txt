A clip-on metronome designed to attach to a music stand. A compact rectangular housing carries a spring-loaded clip arm at its rear that pivots on a revolute joint at the housing back face. A short pendulum rod with a cylindrical sliding weight protrudes from the housing top. Pendulum swings on a revolute at the housing-top pivot; weight slides prismatically along the rod; clip arm pivots on a revolute at the housing rear; winding key on the housing side rotates continuously.
