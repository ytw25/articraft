A DJ-style over-ear headphone set with one-sided cue swivel and folding cup arms. The headband arches over broad fork yokes, and each cup is mounted between the fork arms with a swivel pivot plus a fold joint above the fork. Each cup rotates within its fork, and each fold joint rotates so the cups can fold inward against the headband. Keep each earcup clipped between the fork arms so the cup stays captured while swiveling and folding.
