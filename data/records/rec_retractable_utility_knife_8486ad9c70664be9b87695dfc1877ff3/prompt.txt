A metal-body retractable utility knife with a top thumb slider and a lock wheel. The rigid handle shell contains a straight blade carrier in a central channel, and the lock wheel is mounted in the side wall near the slider. The blade carrier slides prismatically along the handle axis, and the lock wheel rotates on its own local axis in the side of the shell. Keep the blade carrier clipped inside the central handle channel so it slides without separating from the knife body.
