A trumpet case with a compact rectangular clamshell body. The deeper lower shell carries the instrument cavity, the shallow lid is hinged along the long back edge, and two front draw latches sit beside a center carry handle. The lid rotates on a rear horizontal hinge axis, and the two draw latches rotate on their own small hinge pivots at the front edge. Keep the lid clipped to the long rear hinge line so the upper shell stays attached to the lower shell through opening.
