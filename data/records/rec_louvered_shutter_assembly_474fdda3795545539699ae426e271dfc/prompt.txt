A vent shutter insert with one fixed perimeter frame and a short bank of operable louvers. The louvers are mounted between the side rails on small end pivots, and a side-mounted control knob drives the internal linkage. The louvers rotate on horizontal axes at their ends, and the side control knob rotates on its local axis to actuate the louver linkage. Keep the operable louver bank clipped into the side rails so the blades stay captured while the control knob drives them.
