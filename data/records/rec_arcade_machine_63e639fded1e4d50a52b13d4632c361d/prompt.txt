A redemption arcade cabinet with a broad front body, a sloped control deck, and a lockable lower prize door beneath the play window, plus one large illuminated push button at the center of the deck. The lower prize door remains a real access panel in the cabinet face and the push button is a separate raised part in the deck. The prize door rotates on a vertical side hinge, and the push button moves prismatically along its local press axis. Family-entertainment-center scale.
