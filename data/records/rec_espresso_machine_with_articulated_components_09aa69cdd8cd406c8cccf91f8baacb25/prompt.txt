A cafe single-group espresso machine with a tall body, a front group head, a steam wand on one side, and a hot-water wand on the other side. Two rotary valve wheels are mounted on the front upper face above a long drip tray. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, both wands rotate on their own support pivots, the two valve wheels rotate about their own short front-facing shaft axes, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along the base.
