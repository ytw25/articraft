A big-wheel commuter kick scooter with a long deck, a front suspension fork, and a side kickstand. The front wheel is carried by the steerable fork and column, the rear wheel sits at the deck tail under a fender brake, and the kickstand folds from the underside of the deck. The front assembly yaws about the steering axis, both wheels spin on their axles, the rear fender brake rotates on its front hinge, and the kickstand rotates on its small hinge mount. Keep the front wheel clipped between the fork legs so the steered front assembly stays supported through yaw motion.
