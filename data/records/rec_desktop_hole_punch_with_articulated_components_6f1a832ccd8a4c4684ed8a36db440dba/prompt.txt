A high-capacity desktop hole punch with a deep throat base, a long lever handle, a side locking latch, and a bottom waste tray. The latch sits near the rear corner of the body where the handle parks in the closed position. The handle rotates on a rear transverse hinge, the side latch rotates on its own small side-facing pivot axis, and the waste tray slides prismatically outward along a longitudinal underside guide.
