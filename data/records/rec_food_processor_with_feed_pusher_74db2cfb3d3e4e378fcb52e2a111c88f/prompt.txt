A tall family food processor with a handled bowl, a large main feed tube, a secondary narrow ingredient tube, and a front control strip with a ring dial plus two start-stop buttons. The main pusher and the small secondary pusher each slide prismatically in their own guides, the cutter disc rotates continuously on the vertical shaft, and the ring dial rotates continuously. Both start-stop buttons are separate prismatic push controls. The bowl and both feed tubes should be modeled as hollow shell parts with an open processing cavity above the disc. Family countertop scale.
