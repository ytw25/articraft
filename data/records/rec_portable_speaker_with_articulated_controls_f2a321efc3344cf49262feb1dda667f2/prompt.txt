A tabletop Bluetooth speaker with a low rectangular body, a folding top handle, and a front control bank with discrete buttons and a volume wheel, plus three separate front buttons. the front panel keeps the controls readable instead of flattening them into one surface, and the buttons sit as distinct parts in the control face. The handle rotates on two side pivots and the volume wheel rotates continuously on its axle, and the three buttons depress independently as small prismatic push controls. Desktop speaker scale.
