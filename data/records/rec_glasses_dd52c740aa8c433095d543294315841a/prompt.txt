A pair of browline glasses with a heavier upper frame bar and thin lower rims. The front frame carries two temple arms on spring hinges at the outer corners, and small nose-pad brackets project from the bridge. Each temple rotates on a side hinge with an additional spring-loaded hinge segment, and each nose pad rotates on its small support joint. Keep each spring-hinge temple clipped to the outer hinge block rather than drifting away from the browline frame during folding.
