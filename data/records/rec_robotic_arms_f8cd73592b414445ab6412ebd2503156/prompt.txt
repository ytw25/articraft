Design Robotic arms as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a base pedestal feeding a shoulder-elbow-wrist chain with clear joint cartridges. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Make axis order and joint offsets readable so motion paths are unambiguous. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
