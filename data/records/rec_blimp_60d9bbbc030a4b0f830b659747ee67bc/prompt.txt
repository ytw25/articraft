A utility blimp with a thick envelope, a compact gondola, twin engine pods, and a boxy tail cruciform, plus vectoring engine pylons beside the gondola. the gondola hangs from visible support struts below the hull centerline, and the engine pods stay visibly carried by those pylons. The propellers rotate continuously on their pod shafts, the rudder rotates on a vertical hinge line in the rear fin, and the elevators rotate on horizontal hinges in the tailplanes, and the engine pods rotate on short pivots at the pylon ends. Workhorse airship scale.
