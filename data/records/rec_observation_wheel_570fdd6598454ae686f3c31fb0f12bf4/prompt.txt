A waterfront observation wheel with a hub-and-spoke wheel and fully enclosed capsules. The wheel stands on two inclined side legs tied by a central axle support, and each capsule is mounted to the rim on a small yoke. The main wheel rotates continuously about the horizontal axle, and each capsule rotates on its yoke pivot to remain upright. Keep each capsule clipped to its yoke pivot at the rim so it stays supported during wheel rotation.
