A wide-feed juicer with a broad rectangular base, a large clear lid, and a wide pusher block that moves vertically through the top feed opening. A hinged secondary flap covers the top of the chute when the pusher is removed. The pusher slides prismatically along the chute guide, the main lid rotates on a rear hinge, the secondary flap rotates on a small top hinge at the chute rim, and the internal basket rotates continuously about the vertical motor axis.
