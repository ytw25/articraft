A compact point-of-sale register with a rectangular drawer base, an angled control pod, and a rear operator screen on a swivel mount, plus a full keypad with large department keys and smaller number keys. the upper pod stays visibly separate from the drawer housing, and the key field is visibly divided into separate buttons instead of one molded pad. The drawer translates prismatically on straight runners, the screen swivels on a vertical revolute joint, and the screen tilts on a horizontal hinge at the head of its mount, and all visible keys articulate independently as small prismatic push controls. Small shop-counter scale.
