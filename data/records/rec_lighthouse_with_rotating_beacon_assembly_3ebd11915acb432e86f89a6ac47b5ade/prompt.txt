A harbor lighthouse with an octagonal lantern room on a short cylindrical tower. The beacon sits on a central pedestal inside the lantern glazing, and a narrow gallery gate is mounted in the guard rail outside the lantern. The beacon rotates continuously about a vertical axis on its pedestal, and the gallery gate rotates on a vertical hinge axis at the rail. Keep the beacon assembly clipped to its central pedestal in the lantern room so the rotating light stays seated.
