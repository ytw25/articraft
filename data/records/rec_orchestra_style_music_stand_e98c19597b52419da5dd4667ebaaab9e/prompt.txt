A conductor's music stand with a broad desk, a twin-post support mast, and a heavy floor base. The two posts are matched supports for the same desk assembly and a clamp knob locks the height carriage on one post collar. The height carriage slides prismatically along the twin-post mast, the desk rotates on a horizontal tilt hinge at the carriage head, and the clamp knob rotates continuously on its threaded axis. Conductor-stand scale.
