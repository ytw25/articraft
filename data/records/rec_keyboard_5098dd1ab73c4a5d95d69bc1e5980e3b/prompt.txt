A tenkeyless gaming keyboard with a media roller and flip-out rear feet. The shallow rectangular case carries a dense key grid across the top plate, a small roller at the upper right corner, and two folding support feet under the back edge. The individual keycaps move as short vertical prismatic plungers, the media roller rotates about its local axis, and the two rear feet rotate on small hinge axes under the case. Keep the two rear feet clipped to their hinge mounts under the case so they fold without separating from the keyboard body.
