Model Simple ironing board as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a board deck on scissor legs with a lockable open stance, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Scissor action and lock points should explain the supported stance. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
