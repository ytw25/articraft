Design Sliding window as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a framed window with one sliding sash in channel guides. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Sash motion should stay in channels with believable overlaps and stops. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
