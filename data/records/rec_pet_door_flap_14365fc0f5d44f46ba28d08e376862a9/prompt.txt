A wall-tunnel pet door with an outer flap, an inner flap, and a selector dial on the trim frame. The two flaps are mounted at opposite ends of the tunnel on separate top hinges, and the dial sits on the inner frame face. Each flap rotates on its own horizontal upper hinge axis, and the selector dial rotates on its local axis in the frame. Keep both flaps clipped to their separate top hinge lines inside the tunnel so each panel stays attached while swinging.
