Design Rolling walker as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a tubular assist frame with front casters, hand grips, and brake-ready handles. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Caster rolling and frame-fold behavior should be mechanically believable. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
