A business-jet turbofan with a compact nacelle and rear clamshell thrust-reverser buckets. The main fan and core are aligned on the centerline, and two reverser buckets are hinged near the rear nozzle section outside the fixed nacelle shell. The front fan rotor spins continuously about the engine axis, and the two reverser buckets rotate on their rear-side hinge axes. Keep both reverser buckets clipped to their rear-side hinge lines at the nozzle so they stay attached while deploying.
