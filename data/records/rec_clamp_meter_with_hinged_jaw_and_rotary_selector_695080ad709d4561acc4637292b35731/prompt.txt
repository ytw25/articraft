A compact HVAC clamp meter with a narrow split jaw, a pistol-grip body, a central rotary selector, and a lower handle trigger, plus two mode buttons near the lower face. the display and control face sit below the jaw head with a clear step between the head and grip, and the two buttons sit as separate controls under the display. The upper jaw rotates open on a hinge pin at the head, the selector rotates continuously about its center axis, and the trigger slides prismatically into the handle, and both mode buttons articulate independently as small prismatic push controls.
