A compact miter saw with a turntable base and a front carry handle on the saw arm. The saw head is mounted on a rear pivot above the base, with a circular blade inside a fixed upper cover and a movable lower guard. The blade rotates continuously about the arbor axis, the arm rotates downward on the rear horizontal pivot, the table rotates on its vertical center axis, and the lower guard rotates on its hinge near the blade cover. Keep the lower blade guard clipped to its hinge near the upper cover so it stays wrapped around the blade as the arm drops.
