Design Singleleaf drawbridge as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a fixed support frame and one rotating bridge leaf on side bearings. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Bridge leaf rotation should be supported by heavy side pivots/trunnions. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
