A student monocular microscope with a rectangular base, a curved support arm, and a slide stage with a projecting carriage, plus a diaphragm lever under the stage. the optical head is carried by a visible vertical guide instead of a hidden internal linkage, and the lever is supported by the condenser housing instead of floating under the stage. The head carriage slides prismatically on the arm column, the objective turret rotates continuously, and the stage carriage slides prismatically across the stage, and the diaphragm lever rotates on a short pivot beneath the stage. Classroom microscope scale.
