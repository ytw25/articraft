A folding commuter kick scooter with a narrow deck and upright handlebar stem. The front wheel sits in a steerable fork, the rear wheel mounts under the deck tail, and the stem is connected to the deck nose by a folding hinge block. The two wheels rotate on their axles, the front fork steers on a vertical revolute axis at the head tube, and the stem folds on a transverse revolute hinge near the deck.
