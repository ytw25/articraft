A compact point-of-sale register with a rectangular drawer base, an angled upper control pod, a rear operator screen on a swivel mount, and a front-facing bank of menu keys and numeric buttons. The drawer translates on one prismatic axis, the screen swivels on a vertical revolute joint and tilts on a horizontal revolute joint at the top of the mount, and every visible control key is an individual prismatic push button seated in the console face. Desktop retail scale with a realistic plastic shell, recessed seams, and a slightly stepped control panel.
