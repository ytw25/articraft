A wall-mounted air conditioner with a boxy housing, a side service hatch, and a lower outlet with segmented vertical louvers. The fixed body carries a narrow hatch on one side face and a long discharge opening across the bottom front. The side hatch rotates on a vertical side hinge, the main outlet flap rotates on a horizontal hinge at the front of the discharge opening, and the segmented louvers rotate independently on their vertical pivots behind the flap.
