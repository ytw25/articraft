An office espresso machine with a front group head, a side steam wand, and two small flip-down cup platforms at different heights on the front face. A row of three buttons sits above a removable drip tray. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the two cup platforms rotate on transverse lower-front hinges, the buttons move on short longitudinal plungers, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal base guide.
