A European-style slot mailbox with a flat front door and a narrow lid over the posting slot. The fixed body is a shallow rectangle, the main access door is hinged at one side, and the slot lid is hinged along its top edge. The main door rotates on a vertical side hinge axis, and the slot lid rotates upward on its horizontal upper hinge axis. Keep the narrow slot lid clipped to its top hinge so it stays attached while lifting for mail insertion.
