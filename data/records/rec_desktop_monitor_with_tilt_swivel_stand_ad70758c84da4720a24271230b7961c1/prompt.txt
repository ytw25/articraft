A 24-inch office desktop monitor with a rectangular display shell, a slim central neck, and a flat pedestal base, plus a height-adjustable column and a four-way control joystick under the bezel. the lower bezel leaves room for visible front-panel controls instead of a blank strip, and the control joystick sits as a supported control nub below the screen. The screen housing tilts on a horizontal revolute hinge at the top of the neck and the stand swivels continuously about a vertical axis above the base, and the stand column slides prismatically for height adjustment and the small control joystick moves on a short local pivot. Standard office-desk scale.
