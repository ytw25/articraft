A gym-locker combination padlock with a rectangular body, a U-shaped shackle, and four turning dials along the bottom edge. One shackle leg is captive in the body as the pivot side, while the four number dials are mounted on parallel horizontal shafts. The shackle rotates upward on the captive leg axis, and the four number dials rotate independently on their own local axes. Keep the four number dials clipped on their shafts along the body edge so the wheels stay captured while turning.
