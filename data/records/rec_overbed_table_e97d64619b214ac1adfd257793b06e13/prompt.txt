A compact laptop-style overbed table with a narrow offset base, a slender telescoping column, a rectangular top, and a front retaining lip, plus a smaller reading wing beside the main top. the support head keeps the top centered over the column rather than cantilevering from a hidden arm, and the secondary wing is mounted on a short support bracket at the tray edge. The column slides prismatically in its outer sleeve, the top tilts on a horizontal hinge at the support head, and the swivel casters rotate continuously on their wheel axles, and the reading wing rotates on its own short hinge beside the main top. Small bedside workstation scale.
