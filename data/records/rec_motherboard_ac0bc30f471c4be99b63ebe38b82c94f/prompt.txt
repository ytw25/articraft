A gaming motherboard with a primary PCIe slot, memory slots, and a hinged cover over one storage bay. The rigid PCB carries the socket, heatsinks, and connectors, while a small accessory cover is mounted on a tiny hinge near the board surface. The CPU retention arm rotates on its hinge pin, the memory-slot latches and PCIe retention clip rotate on their local pivots, and the storage cover rotates on its own small hinge.
