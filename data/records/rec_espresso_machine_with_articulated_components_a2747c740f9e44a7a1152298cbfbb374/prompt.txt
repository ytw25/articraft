A cafe-style single-group espresso machine with a boxy body, one front group head, a steam wand, and a raised cup-warming deck over a rear service lid. A broad drip tray sits below the cup area. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the rear service lid rotates on a transverse top hinge, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal front-base guide.
