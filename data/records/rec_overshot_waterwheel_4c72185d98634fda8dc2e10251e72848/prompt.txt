An overshot waterwheel with a side-mounted belt pulley on the same shaft. The bucketed wheel stands between two support bents, and a smaller pulley is fixed outboard on one axle end. The main waterwheel rotates continuously about the horizontal axle, and the outboard pulley rotates with the same axle. Keep the side-mounted drive pulley clipped on the outboard axle end so it remains seated while turning with the wheel.
