A dexterous palm with four fingers and a long opposing thumb on a deep palm block. The fingers are independent three-link chains mounted along the front edge, and the thumb is a separate three-link chain mounted off the side of the palm. Each digit root rotates on a separate knuckle axis at the palm, and each downstream joint in the digit chains rotates on its own hinge axis without coupling to the other digits. Keep each phalanx-like segment clipped to the next segment at the knuckle pivots so the finger and thumb chains stay connected through motion.
