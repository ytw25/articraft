A portable travel router with a compact body, two short top antennas, and a fold-out rear kickstand. The housing is a small rounded box that can stand upright on the kickstand, and the antennas are attached to the top corners on hinge bases. The two antennas rotate on their small hinge axes, and the rear kickstand rotates outward on a lower hinge axis at the back face. Keep the fold-out kickstand clipped to its rear hinge axis so it stays attached as the router is propped upright.
