A weatherproof padlock with a covered key cylinder and a thick U-shaped shackle. The body houses the shackle legs with one captive pivot side, and a rubberized cap rotates aside to expose the key cylinder. The shackle rotates on the captive leg axis, and the keyway cap rotates on its own small hinge axis at the face of the lock. Keep the thick shackle clipped into the body on the captive side so it remains captured during opening.
