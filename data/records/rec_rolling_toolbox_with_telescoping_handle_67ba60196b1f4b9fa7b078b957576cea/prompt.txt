A compact rolling tool case with a rounded shell, a pull handle nested into the back, and a top organizer tray beneath a hinged cover, plus two side latch levers. the organizer tray remains a separate supported insert rather than merging into the main body, and the latch levers bridge the lid and body seams as real parts. The rear handle slides prismatically upward, the organizer cover rotates on a rear hinge, the tray slides prismatically out under the cover, and the rear wheels spin continuously on their axle, and the side latches rotate on short pivots at the shell sides. Small mobile-toolbox scale.
