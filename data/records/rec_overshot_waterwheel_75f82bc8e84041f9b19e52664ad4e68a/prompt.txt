An overshot waterwheel beside a timber feed chute with a sliding sluice gate. The large bucketed wheel is mounted between side supports on a horizontal axle, and the gate sits in the chute above the point where water enters near the top of the rim. The waterwheel rotates continuously about its horizontal axle, and the sluice gate slides vertically in its guide grooves. Keep the sliding sluice gate clipped in its guide grooves above the wheel so it stays captured during adjustment.
