A compact programming keyboard with an extra left-side macro column and a rotary knob above the arrow cluster. The case is a shallow rectangle with stepped key rows, one oversized knob, and two fold-out rear feet under the back. The keys move as short vertical prismatic plungers, the knob rotates on its local axis, and the rear feet rotate on their hinge pivots under the case. Keep the media knob clipped on its shaft at the corner pod so it rotates without lifting away from the case.
