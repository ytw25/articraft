A giant city observation wheel with enclosed capsule cabins on the outer rim. The wheel is carried by broad A-frame supports and a central hub, and each capsule is mounted on a short arm with its own leveling pivot. The wheel rotates continuously about its main horizontal axle, and each capsule rotates on its own local pivot where it connects to the rim arm. Keep each enclosed capsule clipped to its rim-arm pivot so the cabin stays suspended as the wheel rotates.
