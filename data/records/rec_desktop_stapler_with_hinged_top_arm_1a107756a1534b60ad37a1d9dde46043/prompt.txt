A full-strip desktop stapler with a heavy base, a rear-hinged staple magazine above the base deck, a separate top arm carried on the back of the magazine, and a rotating clincher anvil under the nose. The staple magazine rotates upward on a rear horizontal revolute hinge supported by the hinge tower at the back of the base, the top arm rotates on its own rear horizontal revolute hinge at the tail of the magazine, and the front anvil rotates on a small pivot in the base. Standard office-desk scale.
