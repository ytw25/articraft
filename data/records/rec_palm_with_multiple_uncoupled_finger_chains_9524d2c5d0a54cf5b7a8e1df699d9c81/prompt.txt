A robotic palm with four fingers and an opposing thumb, built from straight rigid link segments. The rigid palm block carries separate root knuckles for each digit, the four fingers each use three link segments, and the thumb uses two shorter segments at the side of the palm. Each digit root rotates on its own base knuckle axis at the palm, and the remaining joints of every finger chain rotate on local hinge axes between adjacent link segments independently of the other digits. Keep each finger link clipped to its neighboring link at the knuckle pivots so the serial chains stay captured while flexing.
