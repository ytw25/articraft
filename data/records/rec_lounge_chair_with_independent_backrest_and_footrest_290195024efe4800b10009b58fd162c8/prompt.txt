A recovery lounge chair with a rigid seat shell, a narrow reclining back, and a long leg rest panel. The backrest is supported from the rear of the seat shell on hinge lugs, while the leg rest is mounted to the front underside on its own side arms. The backrest rotates on a horizontal rear hinge axis, and the leg rest rotates independently on a separate horizontal axis at the front of the seat. Keep the long leg-rest panel clipped to its front support arms so it remains captured during independent movement.
