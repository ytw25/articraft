A basic chop-style miter saw with a rotating table, a pivot arm, and a guarded circular blade. The fixed base carries the miter table and rear arm pivot, and the blade motor housing sits at the end of the arm behind a lower blade guard. The blade spins continuously about its arbor axis, the arm rotates downward on a horizontal pivot at the rear, the miter table rotates about a vertical center axis on the base, and the lower blade guard rotates on its hinge as the arm lowers. Keep the saw arm clipped to the rear pivot block so the blade head stays supported as it chops down.
