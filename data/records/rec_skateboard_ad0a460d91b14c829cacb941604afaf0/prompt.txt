Design Skateboard as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a deck with two truck assemblies, axles, wheels, and bushing stacks. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Wheel spin and truck steer behavior should be grounded in axle/bushing geometry. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
