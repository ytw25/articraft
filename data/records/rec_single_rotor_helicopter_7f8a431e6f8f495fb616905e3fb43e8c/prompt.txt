A utility helicopter with a compact passenger cabin, skid landing gear, a four-blade main rotor, and a tail boom carrying a tail rotor. One side of the aft fuselage has a baggage door and the upper deck has a separate service hatch behind the mast. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse axis at the tail, the baggage door rotates on a vertical side hinge, and the service hatch rotates upward on a transverse top hinge.
