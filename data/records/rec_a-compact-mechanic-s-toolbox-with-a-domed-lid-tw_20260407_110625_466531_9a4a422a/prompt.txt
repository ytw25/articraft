A compact mechanic’s toolbox with a domed lid, twin side latches, and a side-mounted organizer door. The main lid covers the top compartment, while a small organizer panel opens on one end of the box body. The main lid rotates on a rear horizontal hinge axis, the two latches rotate on small front pivots, the carry handle rotates on two top pivots, and the organizer door rotates on a vertical side hinge at the box end.
