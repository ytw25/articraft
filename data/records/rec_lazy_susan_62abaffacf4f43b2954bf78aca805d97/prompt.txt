A heavy serving lazy susan with an octagonal top plate and a locking tab at the edge. The broad top sits on a circular bearing ring over a fixed base disc, and a small tab near the rim can engage the lower plate. The top plate rotates continuously about the vertical center axis, and the locking tab rotates on its own small pivot at the edge of the upper plate. Keep the octagonal top plate clipped to the circular bearing ring so it stays seated while rotating or locking.
