Model Tripodmounted device as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a three-leg tripod with head plate and adjustable device bracket, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Pan/tilt/clamp adjustments should be carried on a real tripod head. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
