A compact all-in-one printer with a low rectangular body, a rear-fed scanner lid, a front output shelf, and a sloped control strip with six visible keys around a small screen. The scanner lid rotates on a rear revolute hinge, the main paper tray slides prismatically from the front lower body, the output shelf folds on a narrow front hinge, and each visible key in the control strip is an independent small prismatic push button. A small menu dial beside the screen rotates continuously about its center axis. The scanner cavity should remain open and hollow under the lid, with thin tray panels and believable paper-path volume. Home desktop scale.
