A benchtop teaching microscope with a broad base, a monocular tube, an objective turret, and a mechanical stage with a side carriage, plus a second stage-axis control on the stage side. the mechanical stage remains clearly separate from the supporting base and arm, and the two axis controls sit as distinct coaxial knobs on the stage bracket. The objective turret rotates continuously on the nosepiece axis, the optical body slides prismatically on the focusing column, and the side carriage slides prismatically along the stage guide, and the second stage control rotates continuously on its local axis. Teaching-lab scale.
