A naval remote weapon station with a broad pedestal, an armored weapon cradle, and a side-mounted sensor mast. The pedestal carries the azimuth bearing, the weapon cradle elevates between cheek plates, and the sensor mast is mounted to the upper frame on a short folding hinge. The pedestal rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, the weapon cradle rotates about a horizontal elevation axis, and the sensor mast rotates on its own hinge axis at the upper frame. Keep the sensor mast clipped to its folding hinge on the upper frame so it stays attached while rotating out.
