A dual-group cafe espresso machine with two front group heads, two steam wands at the outer corners, and a hinged service lid over the top rear of the body. Two portafilters mount below the heads and a long drip tray runs across the base. Each portafilter rotates into its group head about a vertical brew axis, each steam wand rotates on its own vertical support pivot, the service lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal front guide.
