A compact industrial multimeter with a squared housing, a knurled selector knob, and a fold-out rear tilt support that spans much of the back panel, plus a side power rocker and three mode buttons above the input jacks. the case keeps a thick protective overmold around the corners, and the side control stays separate from the front button bank.
