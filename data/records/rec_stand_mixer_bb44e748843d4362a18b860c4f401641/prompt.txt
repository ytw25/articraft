A satin-white stand mixer with a low heavy base, a polished bowl on a guided carriage, and a rounded head fitted with a wire whisk. The bowl should move prismatically a short distance at the front of the base, the head should tilt on a rear revolute hinge, the whisk should spin continuously on the vertical spindle, the speed selector on the base should rotate on a revolute axis, and the lock control on the base should translate on a short prismatic axis.
