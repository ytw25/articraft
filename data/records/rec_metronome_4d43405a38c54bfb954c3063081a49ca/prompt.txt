A skeleton open-frame metronome with no outer housing panels. A rectangular base plate carries two vertical side posts connected by a top cross-bar; the pendulum rod hangs from a pivot pin on the cross-bar, fully exposed. A cylindrical weight slides on the rod to adjust tempo. Pendulum swings on a revolute at the cross-bar pivot pin; weight slides prismatically along the rod; winding knob on the base plate rotates continuously.
