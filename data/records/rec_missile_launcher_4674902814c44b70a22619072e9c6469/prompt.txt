A box-canister missile launcher with four square tubes on a pedestal mount. The canister pack is bolted to an elevating yoke, and the yoke stands on a rotating base ring over a fixed support column. The base rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, and the canister pack rotates about a horizontal elevation axis through the yoke arms. Keep the four-canister pack clipped into the elevation yoke so the launcher body stays supported during pitch motion.
