A long-reach desktop stapler with an elongated base, a raised rear hinge support, a staple magazine that opens upward from the rear support, and a long top arm hinged at the back of the magazine. The magazine rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge anchored to the back of the base, the top arm rotates on its own rear hinge at the magazine tail, the follower block slides prismatically along the magazine channel, and the clincher plate under the nose rotates on a small pivot in the base. Mailroom or reception-desk scale.
