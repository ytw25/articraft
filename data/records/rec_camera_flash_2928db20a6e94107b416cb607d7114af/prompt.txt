A hot-shoe flash with a boxy lamp head, a short articulated neck, and a rear control panel below the display, plus a side control strip with individual mode buttons. the head and neck stay visibly separate from the battery body, and the side buttons stay separate from the lamp head and neck geometry. The neck swivels on a vertical revolute joint above the body and the flash head tilts on a horizontal revolute joint at the top of the neck, and the side control buttons each depress independently as small prismatic controls.
