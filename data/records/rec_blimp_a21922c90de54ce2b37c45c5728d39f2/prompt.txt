A utility blimp with a thick envelope, a compact gondola, twin engine pods, and a boxy tail cruciform, plus a small wheeled landing gear under the gondola. the gondola hangs from visible support struts below the hull centerline, and the gear remains a real supported undercarriage attached to the gondola structure. The propellers rotate continuously on their pod shafts, the rudder rotates on a vertical hinge line in the rear fin, and the elevators rotate on horizontal hinges in the tailplanes, and the landing wheels rotate continuously on their axle. Workhorse airship scale.
