Design Screwin light bulb with socket as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a bulb form with threaded screw base and matching socket collar. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Bulb/socket threading should be clear and coaxially constrained. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
