Model Sewing box with simple hinged lid as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a storage box body with a simple rear-hinged lid, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Lid rotation should be constrained by real hinge geometry. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
