A towed anti-air missile launcher with twin tubes and folding stabilizer outriggers. The central chassis carries the yaw base and elevation cradle, and a pair of short outriggers fold out from the sides for setup. The launcher head rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, the twin-tube cradle rotates about a horizontal elevation axis, and each outrigger rotates on its own side hinge pivot. Keep each stabilizer outrigger clipped to its side hinge on the chassis so the support legs stay attached while folding out.
