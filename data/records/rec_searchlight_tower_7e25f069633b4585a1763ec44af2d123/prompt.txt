Design Searchlight tower as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a mast/tower support carrying a spotlight head in a pan-tilt yoke. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Pan and tilt axes should be separated and carried by yoke/bearing blocks. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
