Design Tackle box with simple hinged lid as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a tackle box shell with simple hinged lid and latch points. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Lid and latch action should be fully constrained by hardware. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
