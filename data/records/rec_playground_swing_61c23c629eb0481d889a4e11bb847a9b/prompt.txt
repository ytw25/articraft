A playground bench swing with a short backrest and rigid side hangers. The bench seat is carried below a top beam by two side link pairs, one on each side, so the bench stays level as it moves. The bench swings on the upper revolute joints at the top beam, and the lower ends of the side links rotate on their pivots where they attach to the bench frame. Keep the bench frame clipped to the lower side-link pivots so the level bench stays attached while swinging.
