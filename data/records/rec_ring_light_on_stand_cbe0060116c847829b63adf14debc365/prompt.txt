A desktop ring light with a short telescoping mast and folding tripod feet under the base. The ring head is held by a small yoke on the mast, and three legs fold from a hub at the bottom of the stand for storage. The ring head rotates on a horizontal tilt axis, the mast slides prismatically in its sleeve, and the three feet rotate on hinge pivots at the base hub. Keep the folding tripod feet clipped to the base hub so the legs stay attached while opening and closing.
