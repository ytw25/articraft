A travel over-ear headphone set with inward-folding earcups and slim telescoping headband arms. Each earcup hangs from a yoke at the end of a sliding headband arm, and a fold hinge sits above each yoke. Each earcup swivels within its yoke, each fold hinge rotates to tuck the cup inward, and the two headband arms slide prismatically in and out for size adjustment. Keep each sliding headband arm clipped inside its sleeve so the size adjusters extend without pulling free.
