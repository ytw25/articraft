A compact on-camera flash with a narrow body, a rectangular bounce head, and a low shoe mount, plus a side control strip with individual mode buttons. the proportions stay realistic for a small travel flash while keeping the head joints clear, and the side buttons stay separate from the lamp head and neck geometry. The flash head swivels on a vertical axis at the top of the body and tilts on a horizontal hinge near the head cradle, and the side control buttons each depress independently as small prismatic controls.
