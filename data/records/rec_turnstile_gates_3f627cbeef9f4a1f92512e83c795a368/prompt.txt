Design Turnstile gates as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a central post with radial arms rotating on a supported spindle. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Radial arm rotation should center on a supported spindle and bearing core. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
