Design Toy model planes as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a model-plane body with wing-tail set and small prop/stand articulation. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Propeller/stand articulation should be simple, mounted, and plausible. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
