A plantation shutter panel with a rectangular frame and a full-height tilt rod. The outer frame is rigid, a stack of horizontal louvers spans between the side stiles on end pivots, and the tilt rod links to the louvers near one side. Each louver rotates on a horizontal axis through its end pivots, and the tilt rod translates vertically in a short guided motion that drives the linked louvers together. Keep the louver blades clipped into the side-stile pivots so each slat stays captured while tilting.
