A professional drafting table with a wide welded base, a height-adjustable head carriage, and a split-top drawing board with a small hinged reference wing, plus a shallow accessory drawer under the board. the smaller wing is carried by the main board frame instead of floating beside it, and the drawer is carried by short runners below the board frame. The main board rotates on a horizontal tilt hinge, the carriage slides prismatically on the vertical support, and the smaller reference wing rotates on its own hinge at the board edge, and the accessory drawer slides prismatically on its guide runners. Professional drafting-table scale.
