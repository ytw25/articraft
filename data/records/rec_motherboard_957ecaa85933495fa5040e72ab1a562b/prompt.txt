An ATX gaming motherboard with four DIMM slots, large heatsinks, and a small chipset fan. The rigid board carries the CPU socket near the top center, expansion slots along one side, and a chipset cooler with a visible fan near the lower area. The chipset fan rotates continuously about its local axis, the four DIMM slot latches rotate on small hinge axes at the slot ends, and the main PCIe slot latch rotates on its own small pivot at the end of the slot. Keep the DIMM-slot latches clipped to the slot ends so the little hinged arms stay attached when opened.
