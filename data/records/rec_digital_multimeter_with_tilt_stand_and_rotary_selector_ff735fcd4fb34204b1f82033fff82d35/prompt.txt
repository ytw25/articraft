A compact industrial multimeter with a squared housing, a knurled selector knob, and a fold-out rear tilt support that spans much of the back panel, plus five visible soft keys across the front. the case keeps a thick protective overmold around the corners, and the keys sit in a neat row below the display window. The selector knob rotates continuously on its axis and the wide rear support rotates on matched lower pivots as one rigid piece, and all five soft keys articulate independently as small prismatic push controls. Handheld industrial test-instrument scale.
