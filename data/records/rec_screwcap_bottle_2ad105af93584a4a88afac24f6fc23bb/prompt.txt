Design Screwcap bottle as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a bottle body, threaded neck, and mating cap interface. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Thread engagement must read as coaxial helical mating, not interpenetration. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
