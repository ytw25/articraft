A narrow shredder with a pullout front bin, two small transport wheels at the rear, and a top control strip with a power rocker and a mode slider. The front bin slides prismatically from the lower body, the cutter drums rotate continuously on horizontal axes under the slot, the power rocker pivots on a short axis, and the mode slider moves prismatically along the strip. A reverse button on the strip is a separate prismatic push control, and the rear wheels rotate continuously on their axle joints. The lower bin should read as a hollow storage container for shredded paper. Narrow desk-side scale.
