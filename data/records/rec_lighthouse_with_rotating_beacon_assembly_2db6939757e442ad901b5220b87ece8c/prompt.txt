A squat pier lighthouse with a broad base, a wraparound gallery, and a cylindrical lantern housing. The beacon drum is mounted on a central spindle inside the lantern, and a small maintenance door is hinged into the lantern wall below the gallery roof. The beacon rotates continuously about the vertical spindle axis, and the maintenance door rotates on its side hinge axis. Keep the beacon drum clipped to its central spindle inside the lantern housing so it stays seated while rotating.
