A utility helicopter with a compact fuselage, skid landing gear, a four-blade main rotor above the cabin roof, and a tail boom ending in a tail rotor. Two engine cowling doors sit on the upper fuselage behind the mast, and a small baggage door is mounted on the aft side of the body. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the cowling doors rotate upward on top hinges, and the baggage door rotates on a vertical side hinge.
