A trainer helicopter with a slim fuselage, skid landing gear, a three-blade main rotor above the cabin, and a tail boom with a rear tail rotor. Two engine cowling doors sit behind the mast, one on each side of the upper fuselage. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, and the cowling doors rotate on matched longitudinal upper hinges.
