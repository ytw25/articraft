A light utility helicopter with a streamlined fuselage, a landing skid assembly, a four-blade main rotor mounted above the cabin roof, and a tail boom ending in a two-blade tail rotor. The main rotor hub carries four blades as one rotating assembly above the mast, and the tail rotor is mounted at the end of the tail fin. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, and the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse axis at the tail boom end.
