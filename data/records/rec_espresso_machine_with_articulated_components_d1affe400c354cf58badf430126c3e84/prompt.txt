A commercial single-group espresso machine with a tall body, one group head, a steam wand, and a top cup rail over side service panels. A long drip tray spans the front below the brew area. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the side service panels rotate on vertical side hinges, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal front guide.
