A capsule-hybrid espresso appliance with a compact brew body, a short milk wand on one side, a top flip-up brew lid, and a pull-out drip tray at the base. A small water tank lid is mounted behind the brew lid on the rear top surface. The milk wand rotates on a side support pivot, the brew lid rotates on a rear hinge, the water tank lid rotates on a rear hinge, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along the lower body.
