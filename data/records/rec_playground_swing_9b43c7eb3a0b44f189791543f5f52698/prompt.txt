A disc swing with a round seat suspended under a single overhead support point. The disc seat is carried by a rigid central hanger stem that joins a short upper yoke below the top beam. The upper yoke rotates on a horizontal pivot at the beam to let the swing arc, and the disc seat rotates about the vertical axis of the central stem beneath the yoke. Keep the disc seat clipped to the central hanger stem so it stays captured while rotating under the top support.
