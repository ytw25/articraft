A thermoblock espresso machine with a slim body, a front group head, a hot-water wand on one side, and a hinged water-tank lid at the rear top. A row of push buttons spans the upper front panel above a slide-out drip tray. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the tank lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, the buttons move on short longitudinal plungers, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal lower-front guide.
