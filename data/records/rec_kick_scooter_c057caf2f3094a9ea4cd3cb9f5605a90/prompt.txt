A city scooter with a folding stem and folding handle grips for storage. The narrow deck carries a rear wheel at the tail, the front wheel mounts in a steerable fork, and the T-bar has short grip sections hinged at the ends. The front fork yaws about the steering axis, both wheels spin on their axles, the stem rotates on its folding hinge near the deck, and the two grip sections rotate on hinge axes at the bar ends. Keep the folding handle grips clipped to the bar-end hinges so the grip sections stay attached in both folded and riding positions.
