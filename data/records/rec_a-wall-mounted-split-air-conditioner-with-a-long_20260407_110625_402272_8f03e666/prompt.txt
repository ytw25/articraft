A wall-mounted split air conditioner with a long rectangular body, a front service cover, and a wide outlet along the lower face. The service cover is hinged along its upper edge, and a horizontal discharge flap spans the outlet with a row of short vertical louvers behind it. The cover rotates on a top horizontal revolute hinge, the discharge flap rotates on a lower horizontal revolute hinge, and the vertical louvers rotate independently on their own long-axis pivots.
