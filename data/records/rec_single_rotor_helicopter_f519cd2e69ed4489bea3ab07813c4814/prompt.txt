An agricultural helicopter with a compact cockpit, a tank-backed fuselage, skid gear, a three-blade main rotor, and a tail boom ending in a tail rotor. Two engine cowling panels are mounted on the upper fuselage behind the rotor mast. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse axis at the boom end, and the cowling panels rotate upward on matched longitudinal top hinges.
