A side-bridge rotary stack with a drum-like lower stage, a cantilever bridge, and a compact upper flange carried at one edge of the bridge. The upper stage should feel deliberately displaced rather than simply perched on top, with its own local housing and support structure. Use realistic mechanical detailing like flanged housings, split retainers, cap screws, and practical clearances between the two rotating systems. The lower drum is a revolute joint about the main vertical axis and the upper flange is a separate revolute joint about a parallel offset axis. Keep every part mechanically grounded with no floating members, and make sure the articulation paths stay clear so nothing clips through covers, stops, housings, rails, or neighboring links.
