A pyramid metronome with two folding stabilizer legs at the base rear corners for use on angled surfaces. A tapered housing carries a pendulum rod with a cylindrical sliding weight above the apex; two flat legs fold outward from the base rear corners on revolute hinges, folding flush for transport. The pendulum pivots on a revolute at the housing-top shaft; the weight slides prismatically along the rod; each stabilizer leg rotates on a revolute at its base-corner hinge; the rear winding key turns continuously.
