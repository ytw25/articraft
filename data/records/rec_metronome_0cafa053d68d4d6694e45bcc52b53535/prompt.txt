A portable pyramid metronome with two folding stabilizer legs at the base corners. A tapered housing on a rectangular base carries a pendulum rod with a sliding cylindrical weight above; two leg extensions fold outward from the base corners on revolute joints for stability on angled surfaces, folding flush against the base for transport. Pendulum swings on a revolute at the housing-top pivot; weight slides prismatically along the rod; each stabilizer leg pivots on a revolute at its base-corner hinge; winding key on the rear face rotates continuously.
