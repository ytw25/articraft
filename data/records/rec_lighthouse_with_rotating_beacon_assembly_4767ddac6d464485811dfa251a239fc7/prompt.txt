A tall striped lighthouse with a narrow lantern room and a vented cap. The light assembly stands on a central pedestal inside the lantern glazing, and a trap door in the gallery railing opens to the stair landing. The beacon rotates continuously about a vertical axis on the pedestal, and the trap door rotates on its hinge axis at the gallery rail. Keep the light assembly clipped to the vertical pedestal axis inside the lantern so the rotating beacon stays centered.
