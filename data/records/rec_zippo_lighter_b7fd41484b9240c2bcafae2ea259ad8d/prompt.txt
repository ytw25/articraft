A rounded-corner vintage lighter with a rectangular shell, a fitted lid, and a tall perforated chimney insert, plus a small cam lever inside the lid edge. the lower shell keeps a realistic seam where the insert nests inside the case, and the cam lever remains a separate supported piece beside the hinge. The lid rotates upward on a side hinge and the striker wheel rotates continuously on its local axle, and the cam lever rotates on its short local pivot when the lid opens. Handheld lighter scale.
