A built-in microwave with a side-hinged door and a pop-out control knob beside a row of buttons. The cavity sits in a wide rectangular housing, and the control area combines one rotary knob with several push buttons. The door swings on a vertical revolute hinge axis, the knob rotates on a front-to-back revolute axis, the buttons move as short prismatic plungers, and the glass turntable rotates on a vertical axis in the cavity.
