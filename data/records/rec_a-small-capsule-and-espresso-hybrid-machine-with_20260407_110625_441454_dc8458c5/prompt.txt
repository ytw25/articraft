A small capsule-and-espresso hybrid machine with a front group head, a steam wand on a swivel mount, and a rear water reservoir under a flip-up lid. The front cup platform can fold down for taller mugs, and a selector knob sits on the side of the body. The portafilter rotates into the group head, the steam wand rotates on its support pivot, the reservoir lid rotates on a rear hinge, the cup platform rotates on a small front hinge, and the selector knob rotates on its own shaft.
