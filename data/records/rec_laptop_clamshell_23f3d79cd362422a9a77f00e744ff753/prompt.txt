A gaming laptop with a thick rear vent section and fold-out rear support feet. The wide lower chassis holds the keyboard and touchpad, and the display lid is attached by a rear hinge spine with two small feet recessed under the back corners. The lid rotates on the rear hinge axis, the keys move as short vertical prismatic plungers, and the rear feet rotate outward on hinge pivots under the chassis. Keep the fold-out rear feet clipped to their hinge mounts under the chassis so they stay attached while rotating out.
