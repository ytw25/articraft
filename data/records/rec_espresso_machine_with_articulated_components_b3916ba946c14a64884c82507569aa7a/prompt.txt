A prosumer espresso machine with a broad front face, a single group head, one steam wand, and a cup-warming top deck with a fill lid near the back. A rotary steam knob is mounted beside the wand on the front panel. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the fill lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, and the steam knob rotates about its own short front-facing shaft axis.
