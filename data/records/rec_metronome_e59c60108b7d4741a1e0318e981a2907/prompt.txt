A cylindrical-bodied metronome with a round housing on a disc base and a vertical pendulum slot cut through the front face. A pendulum rod with a ring-shaped cylindrical weight protrudes upward through the slot; a winding crown knob sits atop the housing. The pendulum pivots on a revolute joint at the central shaft inside the housing; the ring weight slides prismatically along the rod; the crown knob rotates continuously about the vertical top-face axis.
