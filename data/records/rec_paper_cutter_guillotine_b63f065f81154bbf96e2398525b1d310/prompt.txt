A bench guillotine cutter with a separate front clamp lever and a long blade arm. The rigid bed carries the blade pivot at one rear corner, and the clamp lever swings a pressure bar down over the work just ahead of the cut line. The blade arm rotates on its corner pivot axis, and the clamp lever rotates on its own side pivot to lower the pressure bar. Keep the front clamp lever clipped to its side pivot so the pressure bar stays attached while swinging down.
