A compact service multimeter with a tall narrow housing, a selector below the display, and a short flip-out stand, plus a hold button, a range button, a min-max button, and a backlight button below the screen. the body has a modest bumper lip and a clean lower jack panel, and the four buttons are individually readable instead of collapsing into one fused strip. The selector rotates continuously about its central shaft and the rear stand rotates outward on a short horizontal hinge at the base of the case, and the four front buttons each move independently as prismatic push controls. Small field-tool scale.
