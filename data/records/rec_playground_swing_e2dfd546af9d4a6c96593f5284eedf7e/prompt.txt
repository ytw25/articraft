A nest-style playground swing with a round basket seat under an A-frame support. The circular seat hangs from the top crossbeam on four rigid hanger links that meet in pairs at the beam. The swing assembly hangs from two top revolute joints at the crossbeam and swings as a unit, and the seat frame rotates slightly on the lower hanger pivots where the links meet the basket. Keep the basket seat clipped to the lower hanger pivots so the nest seat stays attached while the frame swings.
