A gaming headset with folding earcups and a flip-up boom microphone. The padded headband ends in side hinges above the earcup yokes, and a microphone boom is mounted to one cup shell on its own pivot. Each earcup swivels within its yoke, the two folding side hinges rotate to fold the cups inward, and the microphone boom rotates on its own hinge axis at the cup. Keep the boom microphone clipped to its earcup hinge so it stays attached while flipping upward.
