A tour helicopter with a glazed cabin, skid landing gear, a three-blade main rotor, and a slender tail boom with a rear tail rotor. The left and right front cabin doors are separately hinged, and a luggage hatch sits on the aft fuselage behind the passenger area. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the two cabin doors rotate on vertical side hinges, and the luggage hatch rotates on a side hinge.
