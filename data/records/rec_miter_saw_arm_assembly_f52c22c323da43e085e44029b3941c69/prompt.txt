A workshop miter saw with a rotating table, a beveling head, and a trench-stop knob. The rigid base supports the miter table and the rear arm pivot, the blade head carries a hinged lower guard, and a small stop knob is mounted on the depth-stop mechanism beside the arm. The blade spins continuously about the arbor axis, the arm rotates on a horizontal rear pivot, the head rotates on a horizontal bevel axis, the miter table rotates on a vertical axis, the lower guard rotates on its hinge, and the stop knob rotates on its local axis. Keep the trench-stop knob clipped on its depth-stop shaft so it rotates without lifting away from the mechanism.
