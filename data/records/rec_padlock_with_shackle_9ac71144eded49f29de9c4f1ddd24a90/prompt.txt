A hidden-shackle security padlock with a broad body and a short exposed locking gap. The shackle is largely enclosed within the body, with one captive pivot side and a key cylinder on the front face. The shackle rotates on the captive leg pivot axis, and the key cylinder rotates on its own local axis in the lock face. Keep the hidden shackle clipped to its captive pivot inside the body so it stays attached when swung open.
