A remote weapon station with a missile pod on one side and an optic turret on the other. The upper structure sits on a rotating pedestal, the central weapon cradle elevates between side supports, and the optic pod is mounted on a small pan-tilt bracket beside the weapon. The pedestal rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, the weapon cradle rotates about a horizontal elevation axis, and the optic pod rotates on its small tilt axis at the end of its support bracket. Keep the optic pod clipped to its support bracket so the sensor assembly stays attached while tilting beside the weapon cradle.
