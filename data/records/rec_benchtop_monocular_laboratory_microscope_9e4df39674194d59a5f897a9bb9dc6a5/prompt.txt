A metal-bodied monocular microscope with a deep base, an upright arm, a revolving objective nosepiece, and a rectangular slide stage with two guide controls, plus a diaphragm lever under the stage. the instrument stays compact enough for a real desk or lab bench, and the lever is supported by the condenser housing instead of floating under the stage. The nosepiece rotates continuously about the main optical axis, the body carriage slides prismatically for focusing, and the stage carriage slides prismatically along one stage axis, and the diaphragm lever rotates on a short pivot beneath the stage. Laboratory bench scale.
