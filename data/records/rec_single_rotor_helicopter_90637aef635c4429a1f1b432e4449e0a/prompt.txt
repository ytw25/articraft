An offshore transport helicopter with a long fuselage, wheeled landing gear, a five-blade main rotor on a tall mast, and a tail boom with a tail rotor on the fin. The cockpit has one hinged door on each side, and the passenger cabin has a separate sliding door behind the cockpit. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the cockpit doors rotate on vertical side hinges, and the cabin door slides prismatically along a side rail.
