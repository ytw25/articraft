Design Turntable as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a plinth with rotating platter spindle and pivoting tonearm stage. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Platter and tonearm each need their own cleanly supported pivot systems. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
