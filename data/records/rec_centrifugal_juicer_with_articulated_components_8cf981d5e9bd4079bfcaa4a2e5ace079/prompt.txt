A stainless steel juicer with a deep base, a clear upper chamber, and a tall feed pusher centered over the basket housing. Two short side clamps rotate up to secure the chamber lid, and a small drip tray sits under the front spout. The feed pusher slides prismatically through the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the two side clamps rotate on matched pivots at the chamber sides, the drip tray slides prismatically forward, and the internal basket rotates continuously on the vertical drive axis.
