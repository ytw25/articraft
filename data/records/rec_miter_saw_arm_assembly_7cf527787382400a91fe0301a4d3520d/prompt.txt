A chop-saw style miter saw with a fixed base, a pivoting saw arm, and a vertical work clamp near the fence. The rear yoke supports the arm and blade housing, and the clamp rides on a short guide beside the cutting area. The arm rotates on a transverse revolute hinge at the rear pivot, the table yaws on a vertical revolute axis for mitering, the blade spins on its spindle axis, and the work clamp moves as a short vertical prismatic slider on its guide.
