A noise-canceling over-ear headphone set with oval cups and fold-flat yokes. The earcups are carried by slim yokes at the ends of the headband, and the yokes include a fold joint above the cup pivot. Each earcup swivels within its yoke to lie flat, and each fold joint rotates to tuck the cups inward beneath the headband. Keep each earcup clipped into its yoke pivots so the cups stay attached while swiveling flat and folding inward.
