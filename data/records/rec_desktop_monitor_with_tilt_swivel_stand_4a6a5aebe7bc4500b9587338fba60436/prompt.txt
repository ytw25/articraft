A curved gaming monitor with a wide display shell, a split rear yoke, and a tripod-style base, plus a height-adjustable column and a four-way control joystick under the bezel. the yoke arms visibly carry the screen rather than hiding the support inside the shell, and the control joystick sits as a supported control nub below the screen. The display tilts on a horizontal revolute joint between the yoke arms and the whole stand swivels continuously about a vertical axis where the yoke meets the base, and the stand column slides prismatically for height adjustment and the small control joystick moves on a short local pivot. Gaming-desk scale.
