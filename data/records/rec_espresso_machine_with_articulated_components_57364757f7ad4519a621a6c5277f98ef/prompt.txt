A heat-exchange espresso machine with a large rectangular body, one group head, a steam wand on one side, and a hot-water wand on the other side. A top reservoir hatch sits behind the cup rail and a drip tray spans the base. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, both wands rotate on their own vertical support pivots, the reservoir hatch rotates on a transverse rear hinge, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal front guide.
