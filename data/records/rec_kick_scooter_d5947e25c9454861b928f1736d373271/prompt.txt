A stunt kick scooter with a rigid short deck, a narrow fork, and a one-piece bar. The compact frame has no stem-fold joint, with the front wheel in the steerable fork and the rear wheel tucked close under the deck tail. The front fork and bar yaw together about the steering axis, and the front and rear wheels spin continuously on their axle supports. Keep the front wheel clipped between the narrow fork legs so the rigid steering assembly stays captured during turning.
