A prosumer espresso machine with a boxy body, one front group head, a steam wand, and two small fold-down cup rests at different heights on the front face. A water reservoir lid is mounted on the rear top deck behind the cup rail. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical side-support pivot, the two cup rests rotate on lower front hinges, and the reservoir lid rotates on a rear hinge.
