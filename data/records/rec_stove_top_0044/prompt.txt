A modern induction stove with five circular cooking zones on a flat top and a large oven below. The front control panel has two rotary knobs at the far left, a horizontal row of three push-buttons in the center, and two more rotary knobs at the far right. The knobs rotate continuously about front-to-back axes, the buttons are short prismatic plungers, and the oven door swings down on a lower revolute hinge.
