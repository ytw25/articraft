A folding adjustable weight bench with a low tubular frame, a split backrest and seat pad, a rear support link, and front transport wheels, plus a foot-roller assembly at the front end. the pads sit on separate support brackets over a compact home-gym frame, and the roller pair is mounted on a rigid front yoke. The backrest pivots on a transverse hinge relative to the center frame, the seat pad pivots on its own hinge ahead of the backrest, the rear support link rotates on a lower frame pivot, and the front wheels spin continuously on their axle, and the two front rollers spin continuously on their shared support axle. Compact home-gym scale.
