A conference office chair with a low back, fixed arms, and a five-star wheeled base. The seat is carried on a central pedestal above the base, and the low backrest is mounted to the rear of the seat support with a simple tilt mechanism. The caster wheels rotate on their axles, the caster forks swivel on vertical pivots, the seat swivels on the center pedestal, the gas column slides vertically, and the backrest rotates on a horizontal tilt pivot behind the seat. Keep the center pedestal clipped into both the seat support and the wheeled base so the seat stays supported during swivel and height adjustment.
