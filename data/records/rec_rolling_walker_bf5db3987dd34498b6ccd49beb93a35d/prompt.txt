Model Rolling walker as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a tubular assist frame with front casters, hand grips, and brake-ready handles, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Caster rolling and frame-fold behavior should be mechanically believable. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
