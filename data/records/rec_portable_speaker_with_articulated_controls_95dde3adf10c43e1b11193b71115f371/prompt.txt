A jobsite-style portable speaker with a broad body, fold-flat side handles, and a large selector wheel near the upper front edge, plus three separate front buttons. the body has reinforced corner bumpers and a deep front grille opening, and the buttons sit as distinct parts in the control face. The two side handles rotate on their side hinges and the selector wheel rotates continuously on its front axle, and the three buttons depress independently as small prismatic push controls. Portable worksite-audio scale.
