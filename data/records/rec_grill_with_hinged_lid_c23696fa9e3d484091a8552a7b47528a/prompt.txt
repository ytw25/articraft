A cart-style gas grill with two separate hinged lids over left and right cooking zones. The lower cookbox is fixed to a wheeled cart, each lid has its own handle and rear hinge line, and rotary burner knobs sit on the front control panel. The two lids rotate upward on parallel rear hinge axes, the cart wheels spin on their axles, and each burner knob rotates on its own small axis in the control panel. Keep both zone lids clipped to their rear hinge lines so the two covers stay seated on the cookbox as they open.
