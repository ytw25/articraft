A dual-boiler home espresso machine with a single front group head, a steam wand, a hot-water wand, and a fold-down accessory shelf below the cup area. A rear access hatch sits under the cup-warming rail on the top deck. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, both wands rotate on their own support pivots, the accessory shelf rotates on a lower front hinge, and the rear access hatch rotates on a top hinge.
