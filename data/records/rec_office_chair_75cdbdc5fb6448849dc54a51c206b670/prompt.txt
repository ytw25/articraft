A stool-height office chair with a tall gas column, a circular foot ring, and a small backrest. The five-star wheeled base supports the seat above the tall center post, and the foot ring is clamped to the post below the seat. The caster wheels spin on their axles, the caster forks swivel on vertical pivots, the seat swivels about the central post, the gas column slides vertically for height adjustment, and the foot ring slides vertically with its clamp on the post. Keep the tall center post clipped into both the seat support and the five-star base so the upper chair stays carried by the column during swivel and height motion.
