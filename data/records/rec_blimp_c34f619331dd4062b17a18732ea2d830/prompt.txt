An advertising blimp with a cigar-shaped envelope, an underslung gondola, twin side engine nacelles, and a cruciform tail, plus vectoring engine pylons beside the gondola. the cabin and nacelles are clearly mounted below and beside the envelope instead of merging into it, and the engine pods stay visibly carried by those pylons. Each propeller rotates continuously on its local shaft axis, the rudder rotates on a vertical hinge at the tail, and the horizontal tail elevators rotate on horizontal tail hinges, and the engine pods rotate on short pivots at the pylon ends. Airfield blimp scale.
