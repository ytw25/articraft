A boutique cash register with a compact drawer base, a rounded keypad pod, and a flip-up customer display tucked at the rear edge, plus a numeric pad beside a narrow function-key strip. The two key fields sit in clear zones across the console face instead of collapsing into one molded panel. The drawer moves prismatically on a single forward axis, the customer display flips up on a horizontal rear hinge, and the number keys and function keys depress independently along their own local axes. Small shop-counter scale.
