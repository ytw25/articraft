A hotel-style wall safe with a broad front flange, a compact right-hinged door, a centered combination dial, and a stubby spoke handle, plus a small document flap high inside the opening. The flap remains a separate panel at the top of the cavity instead of a seam in the back wall. The door rotates on a vertical side hinge, the dial rotates continuously about its center axis, the handle rotates on its front hub, and the document flap rotates on a top horizontal hinge inside the safe opening. Hotel in-wall safe scale.
