A glossy enamel stand mixer with a teardrop-shaped motor head, a bright steel bowl on a shallow carriage, and a balloon whisk beneath the nose. Articulation should stay canonical: the bowl moves on one short prismatic slide from the base, the head tilts on one rear revolute hinge, the whisk spins continuously on a vertical axis, a base-mounted speed dial rotates on a small revolute joint, and a base-mounted lock button translates on a short prismatic joint.
