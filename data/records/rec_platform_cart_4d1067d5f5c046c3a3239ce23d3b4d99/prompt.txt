A stock cart with a low deck, twin end handles, and two fixed rear wheels plus two front casters. The deck rides on a rear axle and a front caster pair, and the two upright push handles are hinged so they can fold toward the deck. The rear wheels spin on their axle, the front caster wheels spin and swivel on vertical stems, and the two end handles rotate on hinge pivots at the deck ends. Keep the two upright end handles clipped to their deck-end hinges so they stay attached while folding.
