A multifunction tailgate with a small fold-down loading-ramp flap near the lower edge. The main tailgate is attached to the bed on lower corner hinges, and the narrow ramp flap is hinged inside the inner panel close to the bottom. The main gate rotates on a horizontal lower hinge axis, the ramp flap rotates on its own horizontal hinge axis in the inner panel, and the outer latch handle rotates on its local pivot. Keep the small ramp flap clipped to its lower-edge hinge inside the main panel so it stays attached while folding down.
