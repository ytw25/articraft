A bifold louvered shutter with two narrow linked leaves. The outer leaf is mounted to the jamb by hinges, the inner leaf is hinged to the outer leaf, and both leaves contain shorter adjustable louvers tied to their own vertical control rods. The two leaves rotate on separate vertical hinge axes, the louvers rotate on horizontal end pivots, and the control rods translate vertically to drive the louvers. Keep the short louvers clipped into the side frames of both bifold leaves so the slats stay seated while tilting.
