A commuter kick scooter with a rear swingarm suspension and a folding kickstand. The rigid deck carries the front steer tube at the nose, while the rear wheel is mounted in a short swingarm hinged under the tail and a side stand folds below the deck. The front fork yaws about the steering axis, both wheels spin on their axles, the rear swingarm rotates on its transverse pivot under the deck, and the kickstand rotates on its small hinge mount. Keep the rear swingarm clipped to its pivot under the deck so the wheel support stays attached through suspension travel.
