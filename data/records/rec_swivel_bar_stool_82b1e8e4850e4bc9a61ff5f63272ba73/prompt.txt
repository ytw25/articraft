Design Swivel bar stool as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a pedestal stool with seat stage on a central swivel interface. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Seat swivel should be centered on a supported bearing stack. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
