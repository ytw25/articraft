Design Toaster oven as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a rectangular oven body with front door and shaft-driven control knobs. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Door hinges and control shafts should be obvious and manufacturable. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
