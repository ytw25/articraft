A rounded desktop shredder with a short pullout bin and a top control cluster made of a reverse rocker, a pulse button, and a jam dial. The bin slides prismatically from the lower front, the cutter drums rotate continuously on parallel horizontal axes, and the reverse rocker pivots on a short axis. The pulse button is a separate prismatic push control, and the jam dial rotates continuously on the top face. The lower bin should read as a hollow thin-wall container rather than a solid lower block. Desktop shredder scale.
