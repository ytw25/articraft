A training helicopter with a narrow fuselage, simple skid gear, a three-blade main rotor on a short roof mast, and a tail boom carrying a tail rotor at the tip. The cabin has one front door on each side with exposed hinges at the forward edge. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse axis at the tail boom tip, and each cabin door rotates on its own vertical hinge axis.
