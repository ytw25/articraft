A desktop monitor with a rectangular display, a central stand column, and a flat base. The display is mounted to the stand through a tilt hinge and a portrait-rotation joint, and a row of small buttons sits under the lower bezel. The stand column slides prismatically for height adjustment, the display rotates on a horizontal tilt hinge at the stand head, the display rotates on a vertical-axis portrait joint behind the panel, and the buttons move on short prismatic plungers into the bezel.
