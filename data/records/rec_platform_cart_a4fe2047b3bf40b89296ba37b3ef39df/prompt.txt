A platform cart with a fold-flat rear handle and a front deck extension. The main deck rides on four caster assemblies, the rear handle hinges down over the deck, and a secondary platform panel slides out from under the front edge. Each caster wheel spins on its axle and swivels on a vertical stem, the rear handle rotates on its hinge pivots, and the front extension slides prismatically along its guide rails. Keep the front deck extension clipped on its guide rails so the secondary platform stays attached while sliding outward.
