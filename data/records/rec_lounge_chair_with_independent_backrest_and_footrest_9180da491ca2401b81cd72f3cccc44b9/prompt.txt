A swivel lounge chair with a pedestal base, an adjustable backrest, and a fold-out leg panel. The seat shell sits on a round pedestal, with the backrest hinged from the rear and the leg panel hinged from the underside near the front. The pedestal rotates on a vertical revolute axis, the backrest rotates on a transverse hinge at the seat rear, and the leg panel rotates on its own transverse hinge.
