A handheld electrician's clamp meter with a C-shaped jaw, a thick insulated body, and a broad function dial under the display, plus a non-contact-voltage button near the top shoulder and a range button beside the dial. the body has a pronounced upper shoulder leading into the jaw hinge block, and the controls stay visible instead of reading as molded graphics. The jaw rotates on a top hinge at the head and the function dial rotates continuously on the front face, and the upper button and the range button both move independently as small push controls. Handheld field-meter scale.
