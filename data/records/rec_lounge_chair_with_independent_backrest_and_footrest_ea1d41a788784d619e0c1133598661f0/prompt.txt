A lounge chair on a swivel pedestal with an adjustable backrest and an independent leg panel attached under the seat. The seat shell sits on a rotating pedestal, the backrest is hinged from the seat rear, and the leg panel is hinged from the underside near the front edge. The pedestal rotates on a vertical revolute axis, the backrest rotates on a transverse revolute axis at the seat rear, and the leg panel rotates independently on its own transverse hinge.
