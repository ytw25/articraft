A drafting office chair with a tall gas column, a circular foot ring, and flip-up armrests. The seat and back are carried by the center post above the wheeled base, and each armrest is mounted to the seat side on a hinge. The caster wheels spin on their axles, the caster forks swivel on vertical pivots, the seat swivels on the center column, the center column slides prismatically for height adjustment, the back reclines on a transverse hinge, and each armrest rotates upward on its own side hinge.
