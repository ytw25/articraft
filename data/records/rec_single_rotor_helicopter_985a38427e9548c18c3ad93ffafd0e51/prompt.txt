A corporate helicopter with a broad cabin, retract-fixed wheeled landing gear, a four-blade main rotor, and a tapered tail boom with a tail rotor. The aft baggage bay is covered by a top-hinged hatch, and one passenger door is mounted on the side of the cabin. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the baggage hatch rotates upward on a top hinge, and the passenger door rotates on a vertical side hinge.
