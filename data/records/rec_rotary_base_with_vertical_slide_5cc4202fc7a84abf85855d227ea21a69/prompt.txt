A rotary mast module with a low cylindrical base, a stiff vertical column mounted on the base, and a compact sliding carriage on the face of the column. The rotating lower stage should feel foundational while the sliding carriage reads as a carried axis mounted to the turning structure. Push the realism with flange bolts, machined guide tracks, carriage ribs, stop pads, and believable structure at the junction between base and column. The lower base is a revolute stage about the vertical axis and the column carriage is a prismatic stage along the vertical guide direction. Keep every part mechanically grounded with no floating members, and make sure the articulation paths stay clear so nothing clips through covers, stops, housings, rails, or neighboring links.
