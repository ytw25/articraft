A compact remote weapon station with a small gun cradle, a sensor box, and a manually adjustable shield flap. The rotating base supports the elevation cradle between two side supports, and a narrow shield flap is hinged at the top edge of the front armor. The base rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, the weapon cradle rotates about a horizontal elevation axis, and the shield flap rotates on its own top hinge axis. Keep the adjustable shield flap clipped to its top hinge on the front armor so the flap stays attached while moving.
