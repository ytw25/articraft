Design Undershot waterwheel as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a paddle wheel on a supported axle between side frames. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Wheel rotation must run on a centered axle with side-bearing supports. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
