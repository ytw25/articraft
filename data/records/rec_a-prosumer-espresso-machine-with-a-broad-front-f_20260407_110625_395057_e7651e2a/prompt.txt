A prosumer espresso machine with a broad front face, a group head centered over a removable drip tray, and a steam wand mounted on one side of the front panel. The machine has a cup-warming rail on top and a hinged reservoir lid behind it. The portafilter rotates into the group head on its front axis, the steam wand rotates on its side support pivot, the reservoir lid rotates on a top hinge, and a rotary steam knob rotates on its own short shaft on the front panel.
