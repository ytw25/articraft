A compact kitchen juicer with a boxy lower body, a clear domed lid over the juicing chamber, and a tall feed pusher that travels vertically through a circular chute in the lid. Two push buttons are arranged on the front control panel, and a metal cutting basket is mounted inside the chamber. The feed pusher slides prismatically up and down inside the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge along the top edge of the chamber, the two buttons move on short prismatic plungers into the front panel, and the cutting basket rotates continuously on the vertical drive axis.
