A compact HVAC clamp meter with a narrow split jaw, a pistol-grip body, a central rotary selector, and a lower handle trigger, plus a non-contact-voltage button near the top shoulder and a range button beside the dial. the display and control face sit below the jaw head with a clear step between the head and grip, and the controls stay visible instead of reading as molded graphics. The upper jaw rotates open on a hinge pin at the head, the selector rotates continuously about its center axis, and the trigger slides prismatically into the handle, and the upper button and the range button both move independently as small push controls. Handheld service-tool scale.
