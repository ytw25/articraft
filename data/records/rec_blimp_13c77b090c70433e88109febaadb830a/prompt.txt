An advertising blimp with a cigar-shaped envelope, an underslung gondola, twin side engine nacelles, and a cruciform tail, plus a rear mooring wheel under the gondola tail. the cabin and nacelles are clearly mounted below and beside the envelope instead of merging into it, and the wheel sits in a short support fork under the cabin. Each propeller rotates continuously on its local shaft axis, the rudder rotates on a vertical hinge at the tail, and the horizontal tail elevators rotate on horizontal tail hinges, and the mooring wheel rotates continuously on its small axle. Airfield blimp scale.
