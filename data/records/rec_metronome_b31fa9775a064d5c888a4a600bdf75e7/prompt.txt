A box metronome with a hinged top lid that conceals the winding key. A rectangular upright housing on a flat base carries a pendulum rod with a cylindrical sliding weight from an upper internal pivot; the lid opens rearward on a horizontal revolute hinge at the housing top, revealing a winding key set into the lid face. The pendulum pivots on a revolute joint at the upper shaft; the weight slides prismatically; the lid opens on its rear hinge; the key turns continuously.
