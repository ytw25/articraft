A photo-oriented all-in-one printer with a broad flatbed lid, a lower main paper tray, a smaller photo paper tray nested above it, and a front panel with one knob and three raised function buttons. The scanner lid rotates on a rear revolute hinge, both paper trays slide prismatically on their own supported rails, the control knob rotates continuously, and all three function buttons are separate prismatic push buttons. A small output tray door at the front lip rotates downward on a narrow hinge. The upper scanner section should read as a shallow hollow bay under the lid, and the trays should stay thin and panel-like rather than solid blocks. Wide desktop printer scale.
