A low console joystick module with a shallow circular base cup, an outer gimbal ring, an inner trunnion cradle, and a short central stick capped by a knurled collar. The base should feel like a panel-mounted control with the nested supports fully visible around the stem. The outer ring revolves about a left-right axis through side pivots, and the inner cradle revolves about an orthogonal front-back axis around the same control center.
