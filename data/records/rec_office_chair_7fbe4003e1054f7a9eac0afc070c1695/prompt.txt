A mesh-backed office chair with a five-wheel base and separate seat and back frames. The seat is carried by a central pedestal above the star base, the back frame is hinged from the rear bracket, and the caster assemblies sit under the spoke ends. The caster wheels spin on their axles, the caster forks swivel on vertical pivots, the seat swivels about the central column, the column slides prismatically for height, and the back frame rotates on a transverse recline hinge behind the seat.
