A two-slot pop-up toaster with a rounded rectangular body, two bread slots in the top panel, and a side carriage lever linked to the internal bread carriage. A browning control knob sits below the lever and a crumb tray slides out from the base on one side. The bread carriage moves prismatically up and down inside the housing, the side lever moves on the same vertical prismatic path as the carriage, the browning knob rotates on its own short shaft, and the crumb tray slides prismatically out from the lower body.
