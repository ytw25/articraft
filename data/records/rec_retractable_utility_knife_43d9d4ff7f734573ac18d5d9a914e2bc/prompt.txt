A heavy-duty utility knife with a top slider and a side hatch for spare blades. The blade carrier runs in a straight internal guide, and the small side hatch is hinged into the handle shell behind the slider. The blade carrier slides prismatically along the handle axis, the top slider translates with the carrier, and the side hatch rotates on its own hinge axis in the handle wall. Keep the side spare-blade hatch clipped to its hinge in the handle shell so it stays attached while opening.
