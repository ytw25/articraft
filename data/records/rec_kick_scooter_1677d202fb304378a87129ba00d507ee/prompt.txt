An off-road kick scooter with wide pneumatic wheels and a folding stem. The long deck sits between a large rear wheel and a front suspension fork, and the steering stem folds at a hinge block above the deck nose. The front assembly yaws about the steering axis, the two wheels spin on their axles, and the stem rotates on its folding hinge axis near the deck. Keep the folding stem clipped to the hinge block above the deck so the steering column stays attached while folding.
