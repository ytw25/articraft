A curved gaming monitor with a wide display shell, a split rear yoke, and a tripod-style base, plus a small bank of menu buttons under the lower bezel. the yoke arms visibly carry the screen rather than hiding the support inside the shell, and the controls are separate parts instead of one fused panel. The display tilts on a horizontal revolute joint between the yoke arms and the whole stand swivels continuously about a vertical axis where the yoke meets the base, and the menu buttons articulate independently as small prismatic push controls. Gaming-desk scale.
