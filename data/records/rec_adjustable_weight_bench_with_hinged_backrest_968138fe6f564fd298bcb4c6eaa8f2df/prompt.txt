An adjustable weight bench with a steel base frame, a hinged backrest pad, a smaller seat pad, a rear angle-support ladder, and front transport wheels. The backrest rotates on a transverse hinge above the main frame, the seat pad rotates on its own front hinge relative to the center frame, the support ladder rotates on a lower pivot at the rear of the bench, and the transport wheels spin continuously on their axle. Home-gym bench scale.
