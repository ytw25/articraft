A recessed home wall safe with a square body, a thick right-hinged door, a round combination dial, and a short three-spoke handle below it, plus a small deposit flap above the handle. The flap breaks the door face as a separate panel rather than a stamped seam. The safe door rotates on a vertical hinge at the frame edge, the combination dial rotates continuously about its center shaft, the handle rotates on its hub, and the deposit flap rotates on a top horizontal hinge in the door face. Wall-safe scale.
