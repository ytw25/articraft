A hybrid hand truck with two large transport wheels and a retractable rear caster set for four-wheel cart mode. The upright frame carries the main axle at the back and a small caster module tucked under the toe plate on a swing arm. The large wheels rotate on their axle, each caster wheel spins on its own axle, each caster fork swivels on a vertical pivot, and the caster module folds on a revolute hinge under the frame.
