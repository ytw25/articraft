A family juicer with a tall tapered body, a transparent upper lid, and a long feed pusher moving through an offset top chute. Two small control buttons sit on the top shoulder of the base, and a rear service hatch opens behind the chamber. The pusher slides prismatically within the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the two buttons move on short prismatic plungers, the rear hatch rotates on a top hinge, and the basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
