A drum hand truck with a curved frame and small rear kick-out support legs. The main frame carries two large transport wheels and a lower scoop plate shaped for a cylindrical drum, with short stabilizer legs folded behind the frame. The two main wheels spin on their axle, and the stabilizer legs rotate downward on small side hinge pivots at the rear of the frame. Keep the stabilizer legs clipped to their rear hinge pivots so they fold down without separating from the frame.
