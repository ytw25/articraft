A compact home espresso machine with a boxy body, a front group head, a side steam wand, and a hinged top water-reservoir lid. A portafilter locks into the group head at the front, and a shallow drip tray slides out below the cup platform. The portafilter rotates on the group-head axis, the steam wand rotates on a support joint at the side of the body, the reservoir lid rotates on a rear top hinge, and the drip tray slides prismatically along the machine base.
