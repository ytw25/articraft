Model Service access panel as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a framed opening with a service panel, hinge side, and latch side, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Door swing and latch closure must be obvious from hardware placement. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
