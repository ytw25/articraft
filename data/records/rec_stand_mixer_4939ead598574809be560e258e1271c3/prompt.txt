A black-and-steel stand mixer with a muscular pedestal, a deep bowl carried near the front of the base, and a tall head nose fitted with a flat beater. The bowl must remain on a short prismatic carriage from the base, the head must open upward on one rear revolute hinge, the beater must spin continuously beneath the head, the speed control on the base must rotate on a small revolute axis, and the head-lock release on the base must slide prismatically.
