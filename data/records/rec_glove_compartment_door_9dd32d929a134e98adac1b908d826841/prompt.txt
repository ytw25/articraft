A marine-style glove compartment with an upward-opening lid and a central push latch. The rigid storage box is recessed into a dash panel, and the lid is hinged at the top edge with two small side support arms to hold it near open position. The lid rotates on a horizontal upper hinge axis, the push latch translates inward on its local axis, and the side support arms rotate on their pivots at the box and lid. Keep the lid clipped to the upper hinge and side support-arm pivots so it remains captured while opening upward.
