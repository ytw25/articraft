A mining-style motherboard with many short expansion slots and a simple chipset fan. The elongated board has a CPU socket at one end, a row of compact slots across the body, and a small cooling fan over the chipset area. The chipset fan spins continuously about its local axis, each slot latch rotates on its small pivot at the slot end, and the memory-slot latches rotate on their hinge axes. Keep the short expansion-slot latches clipped to their slot-end pivots so the latch tabs stay attached while moving.
