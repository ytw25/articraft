A heavy-duty electric screwdriver with a broad motor housing, a front quick-release chuck, and a wide trigger integrated into the pistol grip. A separate speed selector switch sits on the top of the body, and a side direction selector passes laterally through the housing above the trigger. The chuck rotates continuously on the output axis, the trigger moves on a short prismatic guide into the grip, the speed selector slides prismatically along the top slot, and the direction selector slides on a transverse prismatic guide through the body.
