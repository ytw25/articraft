Design Stick vacuum with folding joint as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a slim vacuum body with folding wand joint and floor head. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Fold joint and nozzle articulation should be pinned and bracketed.
