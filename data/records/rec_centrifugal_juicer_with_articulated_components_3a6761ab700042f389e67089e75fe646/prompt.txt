A family-size juicer with a broad base, a clear top chamber, and a tall cylindrical feed pusher above the basket housing. A rear pulp drawer slides from the base, and two small dials sit side by side on the front face. The pusher slides prismatically along the chute axis, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the pulp drawer slides prismatically out from the rear, the two dials rotate on their own short shafts, and the basket rotates continuously about the vertical drive axis.
