A compact rotary drum module with a low base frame, a central bearing housing, and a cylindrical drum carried on one supported shaft axis. The rotating drum should clearly belong to the shaft and the shaft should clearly belong to the grounded frame. Push the realism with machined collars, cap screws, end covers, support feet, and believable shaft-to-drum transitions.
