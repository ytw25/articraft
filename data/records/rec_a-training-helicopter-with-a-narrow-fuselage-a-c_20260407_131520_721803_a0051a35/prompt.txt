A training helicopter with a narrow fuselage, a compact cabin, a three-blade main rotor on a roof mast, and a long tail boom supporting a tail rotor at the rear. The cabin has two front side doors, one on each side, and simple skid landing gear below the fuselage. The main rotor rotates continuously on the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously on the transverse axis at the tail boom tip, and each cabin door rotates on its own vertical side hinge.
