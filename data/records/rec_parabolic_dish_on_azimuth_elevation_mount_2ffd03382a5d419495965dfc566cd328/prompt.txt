A compact VSAT dish on a roof tripod with a rear cradle. The shallow reflector and feed arm are fixed to a back frame, and the back frame is mounted to a small turntable head with side elevation pivots. The head rotates about a vertical azimuth axis above the tripod, and the dish frame rotates about a horizontal elevation axis through the side pivots. Keep the compact dish frame clipped between the side pivots on the tripod head so the reflector stays attached during elevation motion.
