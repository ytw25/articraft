A light missile launcher with two side-by-side tubes on an open frame pedestal. The lower pedestal supports a rotating yaw head, and a compact trunnion bracket carries the tube pair with a small control handwheel on one side. The yaw head rotates about a vertical axis, the tube pair rotates about a horizontal elevation axis through the trunnions, and the handwheel rotates on its own local axis. Keep the two-tube launcher clipped through the trunnion bracket so the upper assembly stays supported during elevation.
