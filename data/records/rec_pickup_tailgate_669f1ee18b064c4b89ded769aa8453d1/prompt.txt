A pickup tailgate with a center pass-through door built into the main panel. The main tailgate is hinged at the bottom between the bed sides, and a narrow access door is cut into the inner face and mounted on one side with a small latch. The main gate rotates on a horizontal lower hinge axis, the pass-through door rotates on its own vertical side hinge axis within the gate, and the small latch rotates on its local pivot. Keep the center pass-through door clipped to its side hinge inside the main gate so the small door stays attached during swing.
