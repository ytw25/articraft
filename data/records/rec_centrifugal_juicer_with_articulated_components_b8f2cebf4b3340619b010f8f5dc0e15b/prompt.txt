A compact centrifugal juicer with a short cylindrical base, a clear upper lid, and a slim feed pusher guided through the center chute. Two small control dials sit side by side on the front of the base beneath the chamber. The feed pusher slides prismatically inside the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the two control dials rotate on their own short shafts, and the spinning basket rotates continuously about the vertical drive axis.
