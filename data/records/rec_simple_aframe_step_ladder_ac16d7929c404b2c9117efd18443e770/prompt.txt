Design Simple aframe step ladder as a field-service workhorse. Build it around an A-frame ladder with paired legs, treads, and spread-limit braces. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Spread braces and leg pivots should define stable open/closed states. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
