A sit-down driving arcade cabinet with a molded seat pod, a dashboard-style control console, and a front coin door below the controls, plus a coin-return flap in the door face. The coin door breaks the front of the cabinet as a real service panel and the return flap remains a separate small part within it. The coin door rotates on a vertical hinge, and the coin-return flap rotates on a short horizontal hinge. Driving-arcade scale.
