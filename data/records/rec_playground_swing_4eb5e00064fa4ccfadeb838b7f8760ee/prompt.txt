An adaptive high-back playground swing with a supportive molded seat. The seat hangs below a crossbeam on two rigid side hanger links, and a front safety bar is hinged across the seat opening. The swing hangs from two top revolute joints at the crossbeam and swings fore and aft, and the safety bar rotates on its side hinge pivots at the seat shell. Keep the front safety bar clipped to its side hinges on the molded seat so it stays attached while opening.
