An office guillotine cutter with a long blade arm and a screw-down hold clamp. The base is a flat cutting bed with a fixed fence, the blade arm is mounted at one rear corner, and a narrow clamp bar is driven by a hand knob above the paper stack. The blade arm rotates on its rear corner pivot axis, the clamp bar slides vertically in its guides, and the hand knob rotates on its local axis. Keep the hold clamp clipped in its vertical guides so the bar stays captured while moving up and down.
