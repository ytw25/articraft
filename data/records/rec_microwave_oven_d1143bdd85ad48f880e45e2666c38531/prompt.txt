A commercial microwave oven with a drop-down front door and a single rotary timer dial. The boxy housing encloses the cavity, a glass tray sits on the cavity floor, and the door is hinged along its lower edge rather than the side. The door rotates on a lower horizontal revolute hinge axis, the timer dial rotates on a front-to-back axis in the control panel, and the turntable rotates on a vertical axis inside the cavity.
