A compact precision electric screwdriver with a pen-shaped body, a rotating bit socket at the front, and two small push buttons on the side for forward and reverse drive. A knurled control ring sits near the front third of the body, and a charging-cap cover is hinged at the rear end. The bit socket rotates continuously about the main body axis, the two control buttons move on short prismatic plungers into the side wall, the control ring rotates around the body axis, and the charging-cap cover rotates on a small hinge at the rear end.
