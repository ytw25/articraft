A modern centrifugal juicer with an oval base, a hinged top cover, and a rectangular feed pusher guided in a tall chute above the cutting chamber. A side locking arm rotates upward to clamp the lid, and a mode knob sits low on the front face of the body. The feed pusher moves prismatically along the chute guide, the top cover rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge, the locking arm rotates on a side revolute pivot at the body shoulder, the mode knob rotates on its own short shaft, and the internal juicing basket rotates continuously about the vertical motor axis.
