Design Tripodmounted device as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a three-leg tripod with head plate and adjustable device bracket. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Pan/tilt/clamp adjustments should be carried on a real tripod head. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
