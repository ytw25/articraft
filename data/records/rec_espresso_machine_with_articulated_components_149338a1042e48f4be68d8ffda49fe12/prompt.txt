A manual lever espresso machine with a compact boiler body, a front group head, a steam wand, and a hinged top reservoir lid. A long hand lever rises above the group head and a small drip tray sits at the base. The hand lever rotates on a transverse pivot above the group head, the portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, and the reservoir lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge.
