A kamado-style ceramic grill on a wheeled nest stand. The thick lower bowl sits in a tubular cradle with two wheels, the domed lid is hinged at the rear, and a daisy-wheel vent cap sits on top of the dome. The lid rotates upward on a rear revolute hinge axis, the two transport wheels spin on their axle mounts, and the vent cap rotates on its central fastener at the lid crown. Keep the domed lid clipped to the rear hinge hardware so the heavy upper shell stays supported through opening.
