A medevac helicopter with a tall cabin, wheeled landing gear, a roof-mounted main rotor mast, and a tail boom with a tail rotor near the fin. One side of the cabin carries a wide sliding patient door, and the upper fuselage behind the mast has a hinged service hatch. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the patient door slides prismatically along a longitudinal side rail, and the service hatch rotates on a top hinge.
