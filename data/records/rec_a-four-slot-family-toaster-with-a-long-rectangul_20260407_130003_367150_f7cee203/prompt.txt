A four-slot family toaster with a long rectangular housing, two carriage levers on opposite ends, and a pair of control knobs on the front face. The top panel is split into four bread openings served by two internal carriage assemblies, and a shared crumb tray slides from the side of the base. Each carriage assembly moves vertically on its own prismatic guide, each lever moves vertically with its corresponding carriage, both control knobs rotate on their own short shafts, and the crumb tray slides prismatically out from the base.
