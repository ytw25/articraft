A polished espresso machine with a front group head, a steam wand, and a side-hinged door concealing a removable water tank inside the body shell. A rotary steam control wheel is mounted above a shallow drip tray on the front face. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the tank door rotates on a vertical side hinge, the steam wheel rotates about its own short shaft axis, and the drip tray slides prismatically out.
