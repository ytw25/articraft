A premium multi-function rice cooker with a broad rounded body, a lift-up top lid, and a detachable inner-lid panel mounted beneath the main lid. The front face has a large release button and two small menu buttons below a display area. The main lid rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge, the inner-lid panel rotates on a small hinge beneath the main lid, the release button moves on a short prismatic guide into the front housing, and the menu buttons move on their own short prismatic plungers.
