A countertop centrifugal juicer with an offset feed chute, a clear upper lid, and a slim pusher matched to the chute opening. A small flap cover is hinged over the top of the chute when the pusher is removed, and a side lever controls the operating mode. The pusher slides prismatically along the chute axis, the main lid rotates on a rear hinge, the chute flap rotates on a small top hinge, the side lever rotates on a short pivot, and the juicing basket rotates continuously about the vertical motor axis.
