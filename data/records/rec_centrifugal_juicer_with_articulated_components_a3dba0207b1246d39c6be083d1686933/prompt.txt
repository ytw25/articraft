A countertop centrifugal juicer with an offset feed chute, a clear hinged lid, and a slim cylindrical pusher fitted to the chute opening. A selector dial is mounted low on the front face, and a shallow drip tray is recessed beneath the juice outlet. The pusher slides prismatically along the chute axis, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the selector dial rotates on its front shaft, the drip tray slides prismatically out from the lower body, and the spinning basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
