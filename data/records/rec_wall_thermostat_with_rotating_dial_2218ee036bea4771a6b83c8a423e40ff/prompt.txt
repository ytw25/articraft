Design Wall thermostat with rotating dial as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a wall thermostat body with a center-axis rotating dial. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Dial rotation should be centered on a clear shaft and retainer. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
