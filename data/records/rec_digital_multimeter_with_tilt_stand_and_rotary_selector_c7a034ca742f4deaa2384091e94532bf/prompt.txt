A compact service multimeter with a tall narrow housing, a selector below the display, and a short flip-out stand, plus a side power rocker and three mode buttons above the input jacks. the body has a modest bumper lip and a clean lower jack panel, and the side control stays separate from the front button bank. The selector rotates continuously about its central shaft and the rear stand rotates outward on a short horizontal hinge at the base of the case, and the side power rocker rotates on a short local pivot and the three mode buttons each depress independently. Small field-tool scale.
