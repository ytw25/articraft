A low-profile remote weapon station with a rotating base, an elevation cradle, and a side sensor pod. The compact pedestal supports a yaw ring, the weapon mount sits between side cheeks on trunnions, and the sensor pod is fixed to one side of the upper frame on a short tilt bracket. The base rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, the weapon cradle rotates about a horizontal elevation axis, and the sensor pod rotates on its own small tilt axis at the side bracket. Keep the side sensor pod clipped to its small tilt bracket so the pod stays attached while adjusting angle.
