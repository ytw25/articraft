A capsule-compatible espresso appliance with a compact brew body, a side steam wand, a top flip-up brew lid, and a rear water-tank lid. A small cup shelf folds down at the front for espresso cups. The steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the brew lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, the water-tank lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge behind it, and the cup shelf rotates on a transverse lower-front hinge.
