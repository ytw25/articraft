A compact point-of-sale register with a rectangular drawer base, an angled control pod, and a rear operator screen on a swivel mount, plus a receipt-cover flap above the keypad. the upper pod stays visibly separate from the drawer housing, and the flap is a real top-edge panel rather than a seam line. The drawer translates prismatically on straight runners, the screen swivels on a vertical revolute joint, and the screen tilts on a horizontal hinge at the head of its mount, and the receipt-cover flap rotates on a rear hinge line above the console. Small shop-counter scale.
