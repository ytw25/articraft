An espresso machine with an integrated grinder, a single group head, a side steam wand, and a hinged bean hopper lid above the grinder section. A short brew paddle is mounted on the front face beside the group head. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the hopper lid rotates on a rear hinge, and the brew paddle rotates on a short front pivot.
