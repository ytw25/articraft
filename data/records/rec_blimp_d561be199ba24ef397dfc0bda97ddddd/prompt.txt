A rescue-service blimp with a long hull, a medium gondola, twin propulsion pods, and a reinforced cruciform tail, plus a small wheeled landing gear under the gondola. the rear tail surfaces are thick enough to read as real aerodynamic panels instead of paper-thin fins, and the gear remains a real supported undercarriage attached to the gondola structure. The propellers rotate continuously on their pod shafts, the rudder panel rotates on a vertical tail hinge, and the elevator panels rotate on horizontal hinges at the horizontal fin roots, and the landing wheels rotate continuously on their axle. Emergency-service airship scale.
