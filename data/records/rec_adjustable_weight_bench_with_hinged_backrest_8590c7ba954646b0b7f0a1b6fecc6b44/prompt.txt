A flat-incline utility bench with a rectangular base, a hinged backrest, a smaller seat pad, and a rear ladder support, plus a front carry handle between the wheels. the support ladder sits behind the backrest bracket rather than disappearing inside the frame, and the handle projects from the front crossmember without floating. Garage-gym bench scale.
