A compound miter saw assembly with a turntable base, a downward-pivoting saw arm, and a bevel joint between the arm and blade head. The base carries the miter table and fence, while the blade head is mounted to the arm through a longitudinal tilt bracket. The arm pitches on a transverse revolute axis at the rear pivot, the table yaws on a vertical revolute axis at the base center, the head bevels on a longitudinal revolute axis at the tilt bracket, and the blade spins on its spindle.
