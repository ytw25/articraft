A studio ring light on a caster base with a heavy central column and a side adjustment knob. The circular light head sits on a tilt bracket at the top of the stand, and the wheeled base uses several caster assemblies around the column. The ring head rotates on a horizontal tilt axis, the stand column slides prismatically for height adjustment, the caster wheels spin and swivel on their mounts, and the side adjustment knob rotates on its local axis at the tilt bracket. Keep the light head clipped into the tilt bracket at the top of the column so the ring stays supported while aiming.
