A compact industrial multimeter with a squared housing, a knurled selector knob, and a fold-out rear tilt support that spans much of the back panel, plus a hold button, a range button, a min-max button, and a backlight button below the screen. the case keeps a thick protective overmold around the corners, and the four buttons are individually readable instead of collapsing into one fused strip. The selector knob rotates continuously on its axis and the wide rear support rotates on matched lower pivots as one rigid piece, and the four front buttons each move independently as prismatic push controls. Handheld industrial test-instrument scale.
