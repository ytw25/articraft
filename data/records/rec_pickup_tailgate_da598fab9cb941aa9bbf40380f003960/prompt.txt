A classic pickup tailgate with rigid side support stays instead of cables. The wide tailgate panel mounts on lower corner hinges between short bed-side stubs, and each side stay is a rigid bar linked between the gate edge and the bed side. The tailgate rotates on a horizontal lower hinge axis, the two support stays rotate on their pivots at both ends, and the latch handle rotates on its own local axis. Keep the rigid side stays clipped to their pivots between the gate and bed sides so the tailgate remains supported when lowered.
