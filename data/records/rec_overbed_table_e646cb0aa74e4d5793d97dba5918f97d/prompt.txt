A rehabilitation overbed table with a U-shaped mobile base, a single mast, a split-top work surface, and a side tilt-release handle, plus a chart clip bar at the tray edge. the smaller side wing shares the same support head as the main top instead of floating beside it, and the clip bar is carried by two short supports instead of being fused into the top surface. The mast slides prismatically for height adjustment, the main top rotates on a horizontal hinge at the head, and the four base casters spin continuously on their axles, and the clip bar rotates on a narrow hinge line along the tray rim. Therapy-room table scale.
