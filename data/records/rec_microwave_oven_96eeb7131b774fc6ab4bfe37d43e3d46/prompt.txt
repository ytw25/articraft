A commercial microwave with a heavier pull-handle door and a simple bottom-row control panel. The rectangular housing contains a fixed oven cavity with a glass turntable, and two large selector knobs are mounted beneath the door opening. The door rotates on a vertical side hinge axis, the turntable rotates continuously about its vertical spindle, and the selector knobs rotate on their local axes. Keep the glass turntable clipped onto its center spindle so it remains seated inside the cavity during rotation.
