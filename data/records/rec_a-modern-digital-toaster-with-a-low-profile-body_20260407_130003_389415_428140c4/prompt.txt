A modern digital toaster with a low-profile body, two top bread slots, and a side carriage lever beside a small control panel with three buttons and a rotary selector. A narrow crumb tray is recessed into the lower side of the shell under the slot area. The bread carriage translates vertically inside the housing on a prismatic guide, the side lever moves on the same vertical prismatic path, the three control buttons move on short prismatic plungers into the panel, the rotary selector rotates on its own short shaft, and the crumb tray slides prismatically out from the lower shell.
