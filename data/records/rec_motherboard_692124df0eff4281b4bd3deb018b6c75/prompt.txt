A server motherboard with a large CPU socket, multiple DIMM banks, and a hinged socket load plate. The board is a rigid populated plate, and the socket assembly includes a lever arm plus a metal load plate that opens away from the socket frame. The load plate rotates on a small hinge at one edge of the socket, the lever rotates on its own pivot at the socket frame, and the memory-slot latches rotate on local pivots at the slot ends.
