Design Simple ironing board as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a board deck on scissor legs with a lockable open stance. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Scissor action and lock points should explain the supported stance. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
