A semi-automatic espresso machine with a front group head, a steam wand, and a side-mounted water tank behind a hinged side access door. A selector dial is mounted below the group head. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the water-tank door rotates on a vertical side hinge, and the selector dial rotates about its front-facing shaft axis.
