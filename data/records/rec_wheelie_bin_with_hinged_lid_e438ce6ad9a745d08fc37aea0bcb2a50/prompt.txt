Design Wheelie bin with hinged lid as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a wheeled bin shell with rear axle and full-width hinged lid. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Lid swing and wheel rolling should both be clearly supported. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
