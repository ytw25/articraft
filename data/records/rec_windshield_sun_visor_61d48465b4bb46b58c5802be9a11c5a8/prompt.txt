Model Windshield sun visor as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a visor panel on a roof hinge arm plus secondary swing pivot, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Primary and secondary pivots should be visible and mechanically credible. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
