An adjustable decline bench with a long back pad, an articulated seat, and a front leg-roller assembly, plus a foot-roller assembly at the front end. the frame has a low wheel set at one end for moving the bench around the room, and the roller pair is mounted on a rigid front yoke. The back pad rotates on a central horizontal hinge, the seat rotates on its own hinge ahead of it, the paired leg rollers spin continuously on their support axle, and the transport wheels rotate continuously on the frame axle, and the two front rollers spin continuously on their shared support axle. Full home-workout bench scale.
