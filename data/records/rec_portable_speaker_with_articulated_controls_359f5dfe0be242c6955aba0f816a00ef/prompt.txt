A jobsite-style portable speaker with a broad body, fold-flat side handles, and a large selector wheel near the upper front edge, plus a power rocker and two menu buttons. the body has reinforced corner bumpers and a deep front grille opening, and the rocker stays distinct from the two smaller buttons beside it. The two side handles rotate on their side hinges and the selector wheel rotates continuously on its front axle, and the power rocker rotates on a short pivot and the two menu buttons each depress independently. Portable worksite-audio scale.
