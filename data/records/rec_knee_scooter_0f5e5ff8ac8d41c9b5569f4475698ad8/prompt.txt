A foldable knee scooter with a center frame, four wheels, and hinged handlebars. The rear wheels are fixed under the frame, the front wheels are carried by the steering fork, and the upper handlebar stem folds forward from a clamp block above the steering column. The front fork yaws about the steering axis, all four wheels spin on their axles, and the upper stem rotates on the folding hinge axis at the clamp block. Keep the upper handlebar stem clipped to its folding hinge block so it stays attached while folding forward.
