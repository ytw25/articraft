A pyramid metronome with a hinged front access door at the housing base for mechanism servicing. A tapered wooden pyramid housing on a rectangular base carries a pendulum rod above with a sliding wedge weight; a hinged front panel at the housing base opens on a revolute joint along its lower edge to expose the winding spring and escapement inside. Pendulum swings on a revolute at the housing-top pivot shaft; weight slides prismatically along the rod; front door opens on a revolute along its bottom hinge; winding key on the rear face rotates continuously.
