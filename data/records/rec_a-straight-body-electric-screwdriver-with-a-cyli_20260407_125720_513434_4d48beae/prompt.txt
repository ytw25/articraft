A straight-body electric screwdriver with a cylindrical handle, a front bit holder, and a thumb-operated power slider along the top of the housing. Near the nose there is a rotating torque selector ring, and a small direction switch sits on one side of the body behind the bit holder. The bit holder rotates continuously on the main tool axis, the power slider moves prismatically along the top slot, the direction switch slides on a short transverse prismatic guide, and the torque selector ring rotates around the nose.
