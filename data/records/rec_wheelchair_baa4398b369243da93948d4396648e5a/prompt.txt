Design Wheelchair as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a chair frame with large drive wheels, front casters, and footrests. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Drive wheels, caster swivel, and frame joints should be mechanically explicit. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
