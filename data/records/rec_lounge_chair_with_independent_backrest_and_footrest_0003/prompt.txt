A padded chaise-style recliner with broad side arms, a separate back panel, and a long footrest panel. The base frame is fixed, while the backrest is mounted between the side arms near the seat rear and the footrest is mounted near the seat front. The backrest pivots on a transverse revolute axis with about 35 degrees of recline, and the footrest pivots independently on its own transverse axis through about 70 degrees.
