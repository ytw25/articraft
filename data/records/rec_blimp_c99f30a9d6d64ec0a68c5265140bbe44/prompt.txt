A sightseeing blimp with a streamlined main envelope, a long passenger cabin, and one propeller pod mounted on each side near the cabin, plus vectoring engine pylons beside the gondola. the tail keeps a real cruciform fin arrangement at the rear of the hull, and the engine pods stay visibly carried by those pylons.
