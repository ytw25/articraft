A snap-off utility knife with a segmented blade, a top slider, and a rear blade-breaker cap. The straight body houses the blade carrier in a guide channel, and the cap is mounted at the back of the handle on a small hinge. The blade carrier slides prismatically along the handle axis, the top slider translates with the carrier, and the rear cap rotates on its hinge axis at the end of the handle. Keep the rear blade-breaker cap clipped to its hinge at the back of the handle so it stays attached while opening.
