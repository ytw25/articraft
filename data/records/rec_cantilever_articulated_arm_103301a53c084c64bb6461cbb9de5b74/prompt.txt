A floor-pedestal service arm with a heavy root column, an offset shoulder yoke, a long upper link, a shorter forearm, and a compact circular tool flange. The links are boxed or ribbed and each joint is held in clear side plates or clevis cheeks so the support structure stays legible. Shoulder and elbow are revolute joints about parallel horizontal axes, and the wrist is a separate revolute joint about the forearm's tool axis.
