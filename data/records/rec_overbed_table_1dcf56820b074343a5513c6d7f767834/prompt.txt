A rehabilitation overbed table with a U-shaped mobile base, a single mast, a split-top work surface, and a side tilt-release handle, plus a smaller reading wing beside the main top. the smaller side wing shares the same support head as the main top instead of floating beside it, and the secondary wing is mounted on a short support bracket at the tray edge. The mast slides prismatically for height adjustment, the main top rotates on a horizontal hinge at the head, and the four base casters spin continuously on their axles, and the reading wing rotates on its own short hinge beside the main top. Therapy-room table scale.
