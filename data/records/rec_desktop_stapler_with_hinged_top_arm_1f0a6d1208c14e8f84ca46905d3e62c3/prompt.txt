A compact half-strip desktop stapler with a weighted base, a rounded top arm, a front-loading staple tray, and a sliding paper stop on the base deck. The top arm pivots on a rear hinge over the base, the staple tray slides prismatically out from the front of the upper assembly, the paper stop slides prismatically along the base, and the rear release latch moves prismatically into the top housing. Small office-tool scale.
