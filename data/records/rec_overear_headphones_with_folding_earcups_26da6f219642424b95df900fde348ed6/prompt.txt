A rugged work headset with folding earcups, a padded headband, and a short boom mic. The two earcups hang from side yokes with fold hinges above them, and the microphone boom is hinged to one earcup shell near the front edge. Each cup swivels on its yoke pivot, the side fold hinges rotate to fold the cups inward, and the microphone boom rotates on its own small hinge axis. Keep the short boom mic clipped to its hinge at the earcup shell so it stays attached while rotating.
