A compact roll stage with a rectangular base frame, two aligned end supports, and a cylindrical spindle tube spanning between the supports. The fixed end supports should visibly ground the spindle so the roll axis never reads as a floating cylinder. Use realistic detail such as clamp collars, cap screws, support feet, and practical shaft overhang at the two ends of the rotating member. The rotating tube is a single revolute stage about the supported roll axis.
