A greenhouse vent window with a top-hinged sash and short side scissor stays. The glazed sash fits inside a light rectangular frame, and each side stay links the sash to the jamb to support partial opening. The sash rotates outward on a horizontal upper hinge axis, and each scissor stay rotates on its paired pivots between the sash and the frame. Keep each scissor stay clipped to both the sash and the frame so the support arms remain attached during venting motion.
