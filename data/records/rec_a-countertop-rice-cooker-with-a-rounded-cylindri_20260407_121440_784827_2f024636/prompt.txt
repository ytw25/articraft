A countertop rice cooker with a rounded cylindrical body, a front control panel, and a top lid hinged at the rear edge of the cooker rim. The lid includes a small steam vent cap near the back, and a single cook-warm selector switch sits on the front face below the body seam. The lid rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge, the steam vent cap rotates on a small hinge at the lid surface, and the selector switch rotates on a short transverse pivot in the front panel.
