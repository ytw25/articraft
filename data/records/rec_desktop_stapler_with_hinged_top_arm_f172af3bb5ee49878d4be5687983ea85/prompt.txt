A die-cast desktop stapler with a flat rectangular base, a broad low-profile top arm, a side-guided staple tray, and a reversible anvil under the front tip. The top arm rotates on a rear revolute hinge above the base, the staple tray translates prismatically within the upper body on its guide rails, and the reversible anvil rotates on a short central pivot in the base. Dense desk accessory scale with a practical office silhouette.
