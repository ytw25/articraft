A drop-down pickup tailgate with an inner fold-down work surface panel. The main tailgate is hinged at the lower corners between the bed-side stubs, and a smaller rectangular panel is hinged into the inner face of the gate. The main gate rotates on a lower horizontal hinge axis, the work surface panel rotates on its own horizontal hinge axis within the gate, and the latch handle rotates on its local axis at the outer face. Keep the inner work-surface panel clipped to its hinge inside the main gate so the smaller panel stays attached while folding out.
