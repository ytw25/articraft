A compact juicer with a rounded base, a clear hinged lid, and a rectangular feed pusher moving through a top chute above the cutting basket. Two small control buttons are arranged on the top shoulder of the base, and a rear pulp bin slides out behind the motor housing. The feed pusher slides prismatically along the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the buttons move on short prismatic plungers, the pulp bin slides prismatically out from the rear, and the cutting basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
