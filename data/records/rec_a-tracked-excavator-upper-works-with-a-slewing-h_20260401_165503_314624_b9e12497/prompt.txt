A tracked excavator upper works with a slewing house, a main boom, a stick arm, and a digging bucket at the tip. The upper house rotates continuously on a vertical slew axis above the undercarriage, the boom rotates on a transverse revolute shoulder joint at the house front, the stick rotates on a second transverse revolute joint at the boom tip, and the bucket rotates on a third transverse revolute joint at the stick end.
