A folding service arm with a grounded base bracket, a long first link, a shorter second link, and a small end pad. The proportions should make it feel like a simple two-link reach mechanism with a clear fixed root and a lighter carried distal link. Make it feel real with machined hinge barrels, formed link edges, shoulder bolts, and believable thickness around the base bracket and elbow joint. The shoulder and elbow are revolute joints about parallel hinge axes in one plane. Keep every part mechanically grounded with no floating members, and make sure the articulation paths stay clear so nothing clips through covers, stops, housings, rails, or neighboring links.
