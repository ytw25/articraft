An aircraft-style access hatch with a side hinge and two latch dogs on the opposite edge. The thick outer frame surrounds a slightly recessed panel, and the latch dogs are mounted near the top and bottom corners of the free edge. The panel rotates on a vertical hinge axis at one side, and the two latch dogs rotate on their own local pivots against the frame. Keep the access panel clipped into the side hinge barrels so the hatch stays supported by the jamb through the swing.
