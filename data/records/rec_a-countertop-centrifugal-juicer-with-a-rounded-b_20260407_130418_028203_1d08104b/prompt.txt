A countertop centrifugal juicer with a rounded base housing, a transparent upper lid, and a cylindrical feed pusher that slides vertically in the top feed chute. A rotary speed selector is mounted on the front of the base, and the spinning filter basket sits inside the upper chamber below the lid. The feed pusher moves prismatically along the chute axis, the lid rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge at the top chamber rim, the speed selector rotates on its own short shaft, and the filter basket rotates continuously about the vertical central axis.
