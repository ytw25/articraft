A pedestal-mounted tracking dish with a broad reflector and twin feed supports. The dish back frame is carried by a rear fork on top of a rotating column, and a small locking knob sits on the elevation side plate. The column rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, the dish frame rotates about a horizontal elevation axis within the fork, and the locking knob rotates on its own local axis. Keep the broad reflector frame clipped within the rear fork trunnions so the dish stays captured while elevating.
