A platform cart with fold-down side rails and four caster assemblies. The flat deck is bordered by two hinged side rails that can stand upright or fold flat, and a fixed push handle rises at one end. Each caster wheel rotates on its axle and swivels on its vertical caster stem, and the two side rails rotate on long hinge axes along the deck edges. Keep both fold-down side rails clipped to their deck-edge hinges so the rails stay attached while folding flat.
