A school paper cutter with a wide cutting bed and a spring hold-down bar. The long blade arm is attached to one rear corner of the base, and a narrow hold-down bar is hinged along the front edge near the cut path. The blade arm rotates downward on its corner pivot axis, and the hold-down bar rotates on its own horizontal hinge axis above the bed. Keep the spring hold-down bar clipped to its hinge near the cut line so it stays attached while swinging over the bed.
