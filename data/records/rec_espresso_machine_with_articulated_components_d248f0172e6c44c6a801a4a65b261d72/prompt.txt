A dual-boiler home espresso machine with a front group head, a steam wand, a hot-water wand, and a fold-down cup platform for small cups. A hinged access lid sits over the rear reservoir bay. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, both wands rotate on their own vertical support pivots, the access lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, and the cup platform rotates on a transverse lower-front hinge.
