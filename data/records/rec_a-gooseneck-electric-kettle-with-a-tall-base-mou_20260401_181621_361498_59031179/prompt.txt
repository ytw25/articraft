A gooseneck electric kettle with a tall base-mounted body, a compact hinged lid over the fill opening, and a slim side handle with a small on-off lever and a separate lid-release plunger. The lid rotates on a rear revolute hinge at the top opening, the on-off lever rotates on a short revolute pivot near the handle base, and the lid-release plunger translates prismatically along its guide in the upper handle.
