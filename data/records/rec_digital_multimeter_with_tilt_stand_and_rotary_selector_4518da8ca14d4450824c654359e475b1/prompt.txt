A rugged autoranging digital multimeter with a boxy body, a large display window, a deep range dial, and a long kickstand on the back, plus a side power rocker and three mode buttons above the input jacks. the front face keeps the input jacks low and the control cluster clearly separated from them, and the side control stays separate from the front button bank. The range dial rotates continuously on the front face and the kickstand swings out on a bottom hinge line, and the side power rocker rotates on a short local pivot and the three mode buttons each depress independently. Handheld electrician-tool scale.
