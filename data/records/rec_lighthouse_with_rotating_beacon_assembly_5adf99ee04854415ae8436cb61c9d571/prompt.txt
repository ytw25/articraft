A classic masonry lighthouse with a tapered tower and a glazed lantern room at the top. The lantern room carries a central beacon drum beneath a domed roof, and a small service door opens from the gallery wall. The beacon assembly rotates continuously about a vertical axis inside the lantern room, and the service door rotates on its side hinge axis. Keep the beacon drum clipped to the central pedestal spindle inside the lantern so it stays captured while rotating.
