An over-ear headset with folding earcups and a boom microphone on one side. The headband ends in folding side hinges above the yokes, each cup swivels within its yoke, and the microphone boom mounts to one earcup shell. Each earcup rotates on its yoke axis, each side folds on a revolute hinge between the headband arm and yoke, and the microphone boom rotates on a small pivot at the earcup.
