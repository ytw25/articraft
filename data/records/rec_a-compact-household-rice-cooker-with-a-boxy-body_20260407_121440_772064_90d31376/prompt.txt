A compact household rice cooker with a boxy body, a domed top lid, and a push-button latch centered on the front of the lid handle. The body sits on a shallow base ring, and a removable steam vent cover is mounted near the top of the lid. The lid rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge, the front latch button moves on a short prismatic guide into the lid handle, and the vent cover rotates on a small hinge at the top of the lid.
