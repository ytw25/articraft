A molded plastic toolbox with a broad top lid, one central latch, and a fold-flat handle recessed into the lid. The lower body is a rigid tub, and a shallow removable tray sits inside under the lid. The lid rotates on a rear horizontal hinge, the central latch rotates on a short front pivot, the handle rotates on two side pivots in its recess, and the inner tray slides prismatically up and out of the box opening.
