A compact laptop-style overbed table with a narrow offset base, a slender telescoping column, a rectangular top, and a front retaining lip, plus a chart clip bar at the tray edge. the support head keeps the top centered over the column rather than cantilevering from a hidden arm, and the clip bar is carried by two short supports instead of being fused into the top surface. The column slides prismatically in its outer sleeve, the top tilts on a horizontal hinge at the support head, and the swivel casters rotate continuously on their wheel axles, and the clip bar rotates on a narrow hinge line along the tray rim.
