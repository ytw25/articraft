A transportable satellite dish with a medium reflector on a boxy azimuth base. The rear dish frame carries the feed arm and sits in an elevation yoke above a squat rotating base with a side crank. The base rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, the dish frame rotates about a horizontal elevation axis through the yoke, and the side crank rotates on its own local axis at the base. Keep the rear dish frame clipped in the elevation yoke above the boxy base so the reflector stays supported during tilt.
