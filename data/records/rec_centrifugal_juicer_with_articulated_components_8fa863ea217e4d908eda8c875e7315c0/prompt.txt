A compact kitchen juicer with a boxy lower body, a clear domed lid over the juicing chamber, and a tall feed pusher traveling through a circular chute in the lid. Two push buttons are arranged on the front control panel, and a metal cutting basket is mounted inside the chamber. The feed pusher slides prismatically inside the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge at the chamber rim, the two buttons move on short prismatic plungers, and the cutting basket rotates continuously on the vertical drive axis.
