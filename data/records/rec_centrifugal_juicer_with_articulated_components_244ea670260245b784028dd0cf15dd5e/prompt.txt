A modern centrifugal juicer with a low oval base, a clear upper chamber, and a rectangular pusher fitted to a wide feed chute. A short selector lever sits on the front of the base, and a narrow drip drawer is recessed below the juice outlet. The pusher slides prismatically along the chute guide, the chamber lid rotates on a rear hinge, the selector lever rotates on a short front pivot, the drip drawer slides prismatically out from the lower body, and the internal basket rotates continuously about the vertical motor axis.
