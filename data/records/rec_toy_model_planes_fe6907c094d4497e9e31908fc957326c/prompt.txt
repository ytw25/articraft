Model Toy model planes as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a model-plane body with wing-tail set and small prop/stand articulation, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Propeller/stand articulation should be simple, mounted, and plausible. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
