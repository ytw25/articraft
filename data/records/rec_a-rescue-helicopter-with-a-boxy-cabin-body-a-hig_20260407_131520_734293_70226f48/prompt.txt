A rescue helicopter with a boxy cabin body, a high-mounted main rotor mast, and a tail boom with a vertical fin carrying a tail rotor near the top rear. The aircraft stands on wheeled landing gear, and a sliding side cabin door is mounted on one side of the fuselage. The main rotor rotates continuously on the vertical mast axis above the cabin, the tail rotor rotates continuously on its lateral axis at the tail fin, and the side door slides prismatically along the cabin side rail.
