A warehouse platform cart with a sliding under-deck tray for tools or paperwork. The main deck rides on four caster assemblies, a push handle rises from one end, and a shallow tray nests below the deck on short side rails. Each caster wheel rotates on its axle and swivels on its vertical stem, and the under-deck tray slides prismatically on its guide rails. Keep the shallow under-deck tray clipped in its guide rails so it stays captured while sliding out.
