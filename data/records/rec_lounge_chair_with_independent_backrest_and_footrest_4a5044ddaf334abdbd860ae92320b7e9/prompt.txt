A reclining lounge chair with a fixed seat base, a hinged backrest, and a separate hinged footrest. The seat pan sits on a low support frame, the backrest is attached at the rear side pivots, and the footrest is attached at the front edge on its own linkage-free hinge. The backrest rotates about a transverse revolute axis through roughly 95 to 130 degrees seat-to-back angle, and the footrest rotates upward on a second transverse revolute axis through about 0 to 80 degrees.
