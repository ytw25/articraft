A glass-window air fryer with a vertical body, a pullout basket drawer, and a front interface made of one selector knob and four program buttons around a small display. The drawer slides prismatically out from the lower front, the selector knob rotates continuously, and the four program buttons are separate prismatic push controls. The basket-handle release button slides prismatically into the handle body. The drawer shell, inner basket, and cavity above them should all read as thin-wall hollow parts with real food space. Kitchen countertop scale.
