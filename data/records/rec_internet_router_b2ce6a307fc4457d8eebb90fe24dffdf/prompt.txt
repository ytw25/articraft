A wall-mount home router with two fold-down antennas and a hinged cable cover. The flat rectangular body hangs vertically, the antennas project from the lower edge on hinge pods, and a narrow rear cover swings open to expose the port area. The two antennas rotate on their hinge axes at the lower edge, and the cable cover rotates on its own hinge axis along the rear panel. Keep the rear cable cover clipped to its hinge line so the panel stays attached while swinging open.
