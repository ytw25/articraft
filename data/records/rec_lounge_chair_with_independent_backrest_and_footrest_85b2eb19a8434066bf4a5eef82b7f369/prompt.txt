A cushioned recliner lounge chair with broad arms and a separate front footrest panel. The seat frame stands on short legs, the backrest is mounted to the side cheeks on rear pivots, and the footrest panel is carried by its own front hinge brackets under the seat. The backrest rotates on a rear horizontal hinge axis, and the footrest rotates independently on its own horizontal hinge axis at the front of the seat frame. Keep the footrest panel clipped to its front hinge brackets so it stays supported independently of the reclining back.
