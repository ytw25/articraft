A lever-style espresso machine with a tall body, a front group head, a side steam wand, and a long brew lever above the head. A drip tray spans the lower front below the cup area. The brew lever rotates on a transverse pivot above the group head, the portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical side-support pivot, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal base guide.
