An office chair with a five-star base, reclining back, and an adjustable headrest mounted above the back frame. The seat is carried by the central pedestal, the back is hinged from the rear mechanism, and the headrest is mounted to the back on a short tilt bracket. The caster wheels rotate on their axles, the caster forks swivel on vertical pivots, the seat swivels and height-adjusts on the center column, the back reclines on a transverse hinge, and the headrest rotates on a small transverse hinge at its bracket.
