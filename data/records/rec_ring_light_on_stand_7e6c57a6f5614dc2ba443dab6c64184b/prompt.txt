A selfie ring light on a floor stand with a telescoping mast, a tilt yoke, and a separate phone shelf that folds out below the ring. The phone shelf is attached to the mast on its own hinge beneath the light head. The mast slides prismatically along the vertical axis, the ring head rotates on a horizontal revolute tilt axis at the yoke, and the phone shelf rotates on its own hinge at the mast.
