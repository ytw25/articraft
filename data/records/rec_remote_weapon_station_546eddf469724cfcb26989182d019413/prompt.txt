An open-frame remote weapon station with a ring base, a trunnion-mounted gun, and a roof shield. The ring base is fixed to the pedestal, and the gun cradle sits between side lugs beneath the shield frame with a boxy sight unit mounted to one side. The station rotates about a vertical azimuth axis through the base ring, the gun cradle rotates about a horizontal elevation axis through the trunnions, and the sight unit rotates on its own small tilt pivot. Keep the gun cradle clipped between the side trunnions of the ring mount so the weapon assembly stays supported during elevation.
