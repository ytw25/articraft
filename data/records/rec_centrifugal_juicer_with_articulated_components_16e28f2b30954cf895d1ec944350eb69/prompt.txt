A polished centrifugal juicer with a compact rounded base, a transparent upper lid, and a rectangular feed pusher moving through a wide top chute. A rotating side latch secures the chamber, and the front spout has a small flip-up anti-drip flap. The pusher slides prismatically along the chute guide, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the side latch rotates on a short side pivot, the anti-drip flap rotates on a small front hinge, and the internal basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
