A compact lever espresso machine with a tall central body, a group head on the front, a steam wand on one side, and a hinged reservoir lid on the rear top deck. A long brew lever rises above the group head and a small drip tray sits at the base. The brew lever rotates on a transverse pivot above the group head, the portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical side pivot, and the reservoir lid rotates on a rear hinge.
