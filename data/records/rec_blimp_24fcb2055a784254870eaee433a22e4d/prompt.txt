An advertising blimp with a cigar-shaped envelope, an underslung gondola, twin side engine nacelles, and a cruciform tail, plus a small wheeled landing gear under the gondola. the cabin and nacelles are clearly mounted below and beside the envelope instead of merging into it, and the gear remains a real supported undercarriage attached to the gondola structure. Each propeller rotates continuously on its local shaft axis, the rudder rotates on a vertical hinge at the tail, and the horizontal tail elevators rotate on horizontal tail hinges, and the landing wheels rotate continuously on their axle. Airfield blimp scale.
