A tall household juicer with a broad clear lid, a long feed pusher, and two locking arms rising from the left and right shoulders of the base. A selector knob is mounted at the lower front of the body below the chamber. The pusher slides prismatically inside the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the two locking arms rotate on matched side pivots, the selector knob rotates on its own short shaft, and the basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
