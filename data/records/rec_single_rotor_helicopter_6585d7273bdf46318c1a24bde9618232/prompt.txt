A police helicopter with a compact cabin, skid landing gear, a four-blade main rotor, and a tail boom with a rear tail rotor. One side of the cabin has a sliding access door, and a small rear equipment hatch is mounted behind it. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the access door slides prismatically along a longitudinal side rail, and the equipment hatch rotates on a vertical side hinge.
