A student monocular microscope with a rectangular base, a curved support arm, and a slide stage with a projecting carriage, plus a second stage-axis control on the stage side. the optical head is carried by a visible vertical guide instead of a hidden internal linkage, and the two axis controls sit as distinct coaxial knobs on the stage bracket. The head carriage slides prismatically on the arm column, the objective turret rotates continuously, and the stage carriage slides prismatically across the stage, and the second stage control rotates continuously on its local axis. Classroom microscope scale.
