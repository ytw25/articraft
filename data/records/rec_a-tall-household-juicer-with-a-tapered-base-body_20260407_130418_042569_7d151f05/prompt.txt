A tall household juicer with a tapered base body, a clear upper chamber lid, and a long feed pusher that moves vertically through an offset chute near one side of the top. The front panel carries three small control buttons, and a locking latch rotates over the top edge of the lid to secure it during operation. The feed pusher slides prismatically within the chute, the lid rotates on a rear horizontal hinge at the chamber rim, the three control buttons move on short prismatic plungers into the front panel, the locking latch rotates on a small side pivot near the lid edge, and the internal filter basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
