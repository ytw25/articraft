A box metronome with a hinged transparent front panel. A rectangular upright housing on a flat base carries a pendulum rod with a wedge-shaped weight swinging from the upper internal pivot; the front panel opens on a horizontal bottom-edge revolute hinge to reveal the escapement gear wheel inside. The pendulum pivots on a revolute joint at the upper shaft; the weight slides prismatically; the front panel rotates on its bottom-edge hinge; the side winding key turns continuously.
