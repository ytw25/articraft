A compact half-strip stapler with a weighted base, a narrow staple magazine hinged from the rear of the base, a curved top arm mounted to the rear of the magazine, and a sliding paper stop on the base deck. The magazine rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge above the base, the top arm rotates on a second rear revolute hinge at the back of the magazine body, and the paper stop slides prismatically along the base to set throat depth. Small desktop office-tool scale.
