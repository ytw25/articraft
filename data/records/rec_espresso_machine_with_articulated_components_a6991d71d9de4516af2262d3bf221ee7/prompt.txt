A stainless steel espresso machine with a single group head, a steam wand, a top tank lid, and a front paddle power switch beside a rotary steam knob. A shallow drip tray spans the lower front. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the tank lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, the paddle switch rotates on a short front-facing pivot axis, the steam knob rotates about its own front-facing shaft axis, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal base guide.
