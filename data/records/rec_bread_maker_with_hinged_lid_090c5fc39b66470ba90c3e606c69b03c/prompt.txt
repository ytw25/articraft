A family bread maker with a broad horizontal chamber, a rear-hinged lid, and two kneading paddles on matched spindles inside the pan area. The lid rotates upward on a rear hinge, both kneading paddles rotate continuously on their own axes, and a front selector dial rotates continuously beside four program buttons. All four buttons are separate prismatic push controls. The wide baking chamber should read as a real hollow pan cavity rather than a shallow solid recess. Large countertop scale.
