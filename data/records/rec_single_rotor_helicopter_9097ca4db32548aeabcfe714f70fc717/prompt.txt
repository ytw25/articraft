A medical transport helicopter with a tall fuselage, skid landing gear, a four-blade main rotor, and a tail boom with a small tail rotor at the rear. One side carries a sliding patient door and the opposite side carries a hinged equipment-bay hatch. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the patient door slides prismatically along a longitudinal fuselage side rail, and the equipment hatch rotates on a vertical side hinge.
