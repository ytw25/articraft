A professional shoulder camcorder with a boxy main body, top carry handle, rear eyepiece, long front lens, and a left-side flip-out LCD screen. The screen should use a supported two-part articulation: one hinge swings the LCD assembly out from the side of the body, and a second pivot lets the screen panel rotate on the arm. Include a lens barrel with at least one rotating ring, a hinged media door, and a few small buttons or rocker controls. Make the moving parts physically supported by visible hinges, brackets, rails, collars, or sockets, with no floating controls. Camcorder scale.
