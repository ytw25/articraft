A two-seat face-to-face playground glider on an overhead frame. The central bench frame is suspended by a left and right pair of rigid parallel links from the top beam, with a seat on each side of the center footwell. The bench swings as a unit on the upper revolute joints at the beam, and the lower ends of the parallel links rotate on their pivots at the bench frame. Keep the bench frame clipped to the lower parallel-link pivots so the glider seat stays attached while swinging.
