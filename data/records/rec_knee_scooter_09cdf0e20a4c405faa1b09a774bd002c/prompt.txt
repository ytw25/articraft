A knee scooter with a front basket, four small wheels, and a fold-down parking stand. The frame carries the knee pad deck at the center, the front axle is built into the steerable fork, and a short stand folds from the lower frame when parked. The front fork yaws about the steering axis, the four wheels spin on their axles, and the parking stand rotates on its hinge pivot under the frame. Keep the fold-down parking stand clipped to its hinge under the frame so it stays attached while rotating between parked and stowed positions.
