Design Vacuum cleaner with articulated wand as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a vacuum body with articulated wand segments and floor nozzle. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Wand/nozzle articulation should be jointed with collars and pivots. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
