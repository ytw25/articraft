A premium adjustable laptop stand with an oval-edged base, a slim top platform with a front retaining lip, and sculpted dual-link side arms that hold the platform above the desk. The side arms are arranged as matched upper and lower bars with circular pivot housings at their ends, and the platform has a large central opening for ventilation. The upper and lower bars rotate on revolute pivots at the base brackets and at the platform brackets, creating a parallel-link mechanism that changes the platform height.
