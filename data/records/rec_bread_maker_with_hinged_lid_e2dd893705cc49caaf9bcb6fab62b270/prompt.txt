A tall loaf bread maker with a near-vertical body, a rear-hinged lid, and a stepped control pod carrying one selector knob surrounded by four preset buttons. The lid rotates upward on a rear hinge, the kneading paddle rotates continuously on the bottom-center spindle, and the selector knob rotates continuously on the control pod.
