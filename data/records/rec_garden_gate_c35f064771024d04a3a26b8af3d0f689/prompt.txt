An arched-top wooden wicket gate between masonry posts. The framed leaf has vertical pickets, a diagonal brace, two strap hinges at one side, a thumb latch at the free edge, and a short closer arm near the top rail. The leaf rotates on a vertical hinge axis at the post, the thumb latch rotates on its own small pivot, and the closer arm rotates on its two support pivots as the gate swings. Keep the gate leaf clipped into the strap hinges at the post so the stile stays supported through the swing.
