A tall box-style metronome with an open vertical slot in the front face exposing the full pendulum travel. A rectangular housing on a low rectangular foot carries a slim pendulum rod with a wedge-shaped weight through the front slot; a round winding key is centered on the left side face. The pendulum pivots on a revolute joint at the upper interior shaft; the weight slides prismatically along the rod; the key rotates continuously about its side-face axis.
