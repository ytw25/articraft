Design Service access panel as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a framed opening with a service panel, hinge side, and latch side. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Door swing and latch closure must be obvious from hardware placement. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
