A kitchen juicer with a compact base, a transparent upper lid, and a cylindrical feed pusher guided through the center of the lid. One side of the body has a hinged service door for access to the pulp channel, and a small control knob sits below it. The feed pusher slides prismatically in the chute, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the service door rotates on a vertical side hinge, the control knob rotates on a short shaft, and the filter basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
