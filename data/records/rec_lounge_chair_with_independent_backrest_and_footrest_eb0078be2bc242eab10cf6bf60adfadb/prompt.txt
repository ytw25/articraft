A reclining lounge chair with a fixed seat base, a hinged backrest, and a separate footrest panel. The seat sits on a low support frame, the backrest is attached near the rear side pivots, and the footrest is attached at the front edge on its own hinges. The backrest rotates on a transverse revolute axis at the seat rear, and the footrest rotates independently on a second transverse revolute axis at the seat front.
