A padded chaise-style recliner with broad side arms, a separate back panel, and a long leg-rest panel. The base frame is fixed, the backrest is mounted between the side arms near the seat rear, and the leg-rest panel is mounted near the front edge with its own hinge pair. The backrest pivots on a transverse revolute axis behind the seat, and the leg-rest pivots independently on a second transverse revolute axis at the front.
