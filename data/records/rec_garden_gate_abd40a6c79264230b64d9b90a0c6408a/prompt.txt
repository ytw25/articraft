A low garden gate with a solid lower panel and an open lattice upper section. The single leaf is mounted between square posts on two side hinges and closes with a compact rotary latch at the free edge. The leaf rotates on a vertical revolute hinge axis at the post, and the latch handle rotates on its own small pivot at the meeting stile. Keep the leaf clipped into the side hinges at the post so the panel does not separate from its support as it swings.
