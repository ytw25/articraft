A corporate transport helicopter with a broad fuselage, wheeled landing gear, a four-blade main rotor, and a high tail boom with a tail rotor. A hinged top service panel sits behind the mast, and a side passenger door opens on the cabin. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the service panel rotates upward on a transverse top hinge, and the passenger door rotates on a vertical side hinge.
