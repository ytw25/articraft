Model Traditional windmill as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a tower with rotor hub, blade lattice, and cap/nacelle support, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Rotor/cap stages should be shaft-supported with clear bearing seats. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
