A matte-finish stand mixer with a thick neck rising from the rear of the base, a wide bowl on a compact carriage, and a head fitted with a paddle attachment. The bowl carriage should be a short prismatic stage relative to the base, the head should tilt upward on a single rear revolute hinge, the paddle should spin continuously under the head, the speed control on the base should be a small revolute knob, and the head-lock on the base should be a short prismatic slider.
