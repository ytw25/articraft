A superautomatic espresso machine with a tall front body, a side milk wand, a top water-tank lid, and a hinged service door for the brew chamber. A removable drip tray and a rotary selector sit below the cup area. The milk wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the water-tank lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, the service door rotates on a vertical side hinge, the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal base guide, and the selector rotates on a short front-facing shaft axis.
