A classic flip-top lighter with a rectangular outer case, a fitted inner chimney insert, and a striker wheel beside the wick opening, plus a small cam lever inside the lid edge. the lid and lower case are clearly separate shells with a narrow side hinge, and the cam lever remains a separate supported piece beside the hinge. The lid rotates upward on a side-mounted revolute hinge and the striker wheel rotates continuously about its short transverse axle, and the cam lever rotates on its short local pivot when the lid opens. Small pocket-lighter scale.
