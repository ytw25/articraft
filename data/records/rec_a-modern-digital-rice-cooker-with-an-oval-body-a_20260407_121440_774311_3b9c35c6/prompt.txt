A modern digital rice cooker with an oval body, a rear-hinged lid, and a fold-out carry handle mounted on the side shoulders of the cooker. A row of control buttons is arranged on the sloped front panel below a small display window. The lid rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge, the carry handle rotates on two side pivots at the upper body, and the control buttons move on short prismatic plungers into the front panel.
