A medium transport helicopter with a large cabin, wheeled landing gear, a four-blade main rotor, and a tail boom with a tail rotor mounted near the tip. A sliding side cabin door sits behind the cockpit, and each cockpit side has its own hinged door. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about the transverse tail axis, the cabin door slides prismatically along a longitudinal side rail, and the cockpit doors rotate on vertical side hinges.
