Design Simple drying rack as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a foldable rack with wing frames, hinge links, and hanging rails. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Wing folding should follow clear hinge lines and stop positions. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
