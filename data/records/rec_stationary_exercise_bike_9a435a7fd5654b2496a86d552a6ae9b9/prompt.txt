Design Stationary exercise bike as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a bike frame with crank set, flywheel housing, saddle post, and bars. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Crank and adjusters should be mounted on explicit bearing/clamp features. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
