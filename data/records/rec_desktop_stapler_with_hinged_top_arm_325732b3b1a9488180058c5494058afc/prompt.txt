A die-cast desktop stapler with a flat rectangular base, a boxy staple magazine connected to a rear hinge tower, a low-profile top arm carried on the back of the magazine, and a reversible anvil in the base. The staple magazine rotates upward on a rear horizontal revolute hinge at the hinge tower, the top arm rotates on a second rear revolute hinge at the back of the magazine shell, and the reversible anvil rotates on a short central pivot beneath the stapling nose. Dense desk-accessory scale with a practical office silhouette.
