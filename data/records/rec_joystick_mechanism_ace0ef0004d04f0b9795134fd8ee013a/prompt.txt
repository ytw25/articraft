A low console joystick module with a shallow circular base cup, an outer gimbal ring, an inner trunnion cradle, and a short central stick capped by a knurled collar. The base should feel panel-mounted and the nested supports should fully explain the motion without relying on a decorative outer shell. Make it feel like a real industrial control with cast yokes, machined pivots, trim bezels only where needed, and believable thickness around the gimbal rings. The outer ring revolves about a left-to-right axis through side pivots, and the inner cradle revolves about an orthogonal front-to-back axis around the same control center. Keep every part mechanically grounded with no floating members, and make sure the articulation paths stay clear so nothing clips through covers, stops, housings, rails, or neighboring links.
