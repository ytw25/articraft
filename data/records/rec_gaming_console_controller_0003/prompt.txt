A compact gaming controller with a wide flat body, a D-pad on the left face, a symmetric face button cluster on the right, and two short thumbsticks centered between them. The housing is a rounded rectangular shell in light grey plastic with glossy face and matte back. Each thumbstick pivots on a two-axis revolute gimbal post sunk flush into the face surface. Left and right shoulder bumpers each hinge from the top edge of the housing on individual revolute joints. Left and right trigger paddles hinge from the top rear edge with a shallow rearward arc on revolute joints. Build clean rigid shell geometry with consistent wall thickness and accurate button cluster spacing.
