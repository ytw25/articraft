A wide-mouth centrifugal juicer with a low rectangular base, a large clear lid, and a broad cylindrical feed pusher centered in the top chute. Matched left and right locking arms rise from the body shoulders to clamp the lid in place. The feed pusher slides prismatically along the chute axis, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the two locking arms rotate on matched side pivots, and the internal basket rotates continuously about the vertical drive axis.
