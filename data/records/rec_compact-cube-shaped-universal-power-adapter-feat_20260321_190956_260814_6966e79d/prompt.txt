 Compact cube-shaped universal power adapter featuring a modular mechanical interlocking system where three parallel vertical thumb-switches labeled UK, USA/AUS, and EURO are rigged with prismatic translation joints to drive internal deployment logic. Each slider is connected to an articulated linkage rod that translates linear downward motion into 90-degree rotational torque for the bottom-mounted plug heads, which are mounted on revolute hinge joints. A spring-loaded horizontal safety interlock bar sits behind the tracks to mechanically block the path of the other two sliders once one is engaged, ensuring only one plug type can deploy at a time. The USA/AUS prongs include an additional manual swivel joint at their base to rotate between parallel and slanted configurations. The interior architecture utilizes a layered system of stamped phosphor bronze busbars for constant electrical contact instead of loose wires, alongside a vertically oriented PCBA mounted behind the side-facing 2x USB-C and 2x USB-A ports. The top universal female socket is backed by a spring-tensioned dual-leaf safety shutter that requires a synchronized lateral sliding joint to open, while the side panel features precisely labeled C1 5V/3A and C2 5V/3A ports with a 3.4A MAX engraving centered below the horizontal USB-A stack.
