Model Tilting fan as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a base, guarded fan head, and side pivots for tilt control, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Fan tilt and rotor spin should be tied to explicit pivots and hubs. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
