A basement dehumidifier with a fold-up top handle, a front pullout bucket, and a control pod made of a timer dial, a side power rocker, and two moisture buttons. The bucket slides prismatically from the body, the top handle rotates on matched pivots, the timer dial rotates continuously, and the side power rocker pivots on a short axis. Both moisture buttons are separate prismatic push controls. The front bucket and surrounding cavity should remain clearly hollow and open rather than reading as solid mass. Utility-room scale.
