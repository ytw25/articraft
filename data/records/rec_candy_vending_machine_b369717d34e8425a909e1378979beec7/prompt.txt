A pedestal gumball machine with a spherical clear globe, a cast-metal coin mechanism, and a lower retrieval chute at the front. The globe sits above the mechanism body on a short neck, and a hinged refill lid caps the top opening instead of reading as a sealed sphere. The refill lid rotates on a rear hinge at the globe crown, the dispense knob rotates continuously on a horizontal shaft through the coin mechanism, and the chute flap rotates on a small lower hinge. Lobby vending-machine scale.
