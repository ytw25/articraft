A lounge chair with a star base, a high back, and a separate ottoman-like foot panel attached under the seat. The seat shell is fixed to a swiveling pedestal, and the back and front support panel are each mounted on their own hinges. The base rotates on a vertical axis, the backrest rotates on a transverse hinge at the shell rear, and the foot panel rotates on its own transverse hinge near the shell underside.
