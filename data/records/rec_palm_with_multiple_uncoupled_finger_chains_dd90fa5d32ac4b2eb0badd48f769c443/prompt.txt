An anthropomorphic robotic palm with four fingers and a thumb, where the long fingers also have splay at the base. The palm block carries separate base knuckles for every digit, the four long fingers each have three serial link segments after the base, and the thumb has a shorter two-link chain. Each finger root rotates on a local base axis at the palm for splay, and every joint farther out in each chain rotates on its own hinge axis independently. Keep the long finger chains clipped together at their knuckle pivots so the links remain captured during splay and flexion.
