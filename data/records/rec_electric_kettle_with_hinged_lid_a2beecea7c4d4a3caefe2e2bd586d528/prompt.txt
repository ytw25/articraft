A wide family electric kettle with a broad body, a separate heating base, a domed rear-hinged lid, a release button on the top of the handle, and a small illuminated on-off switch at the bottom of the handle above the base socket. The kettle body lifts from the base on one vertical prismatic joint, the lid rotates upward on a rear hinge, the release button is a small prismatic push control, and the on-off switch rotates on a short revolute pivot. Three visible temperature buttons on the heating base articulate independently as small prismatic push buttons. The kettle should be a believable hollow container with thin shell walls and open interior volume, not a solid block. Standard countertop kettle scale.
