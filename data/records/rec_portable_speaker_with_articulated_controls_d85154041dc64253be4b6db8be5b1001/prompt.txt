A tabletop Bluetooth speaker with a low rectangular body, a folding top handle, and a front control bank with discrete buttons and a volume wheel, plus a power rocker and two menu buttons. the front panel keeps the controls readable instead of flattening them into one surface, and the rocker stays distinct from the two smaller buttons beside it. The handle rotates on two side pivots and the volume wheel rotates continuously on its axle, and the power rocker rotates on a short pivot and the two menu buttons each depress independently.
