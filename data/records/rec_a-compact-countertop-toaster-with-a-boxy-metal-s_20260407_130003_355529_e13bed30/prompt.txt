A compact countertop toaster with a boxy metal shell, a single wide carriage lever on the front corner, and two push buttons arranged above a small timer dial. The top has two parallel bread slots, and a shallow crumb tray is mounted in a lower front slot. The internal bread carriage translates vertically on a prismatic guide, the lever follows a vertical prismatic motion along the side of the housing, the two buttons move on short prismatic plungers into the front panel, the timer dial rotates on a short front-facing shaft, and the crumb tray slides prismatically out from the base.
