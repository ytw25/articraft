A tripod missile launcher with a single tube and a compact elevation bracket. The launcher tube is clamped to a small cradle head above the tripod apex, and the head sits on a simple yaw plate. The tripod head rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, and the launcher tube cradle rotates about a horizontal elevation axis through the side trunnions. Keep the single-tube cradle clipped to the side trunnions of the tripod head so it remains captured while elevating.
