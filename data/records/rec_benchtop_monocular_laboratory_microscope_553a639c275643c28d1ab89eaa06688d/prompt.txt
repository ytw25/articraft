A metal-bodied monocular microscope with a deep base, an upright arm, a revolving objective nosepiece, and a rectangular slide stage with two guide controls, plus a second stage-axis control on the stage side. the instrument stays compact enough for a real desk or lab bench, and the two axis controls sit as distinct coaxial knobs on the stage bracket.
