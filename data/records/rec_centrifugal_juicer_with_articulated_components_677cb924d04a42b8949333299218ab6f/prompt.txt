A family juicer with a smooth rounded base, a clear chamber lid, and a cylindrical feed pusher above the cutter basket. A removable drip drawer slides out from the front of the base, and two small rocker switches are mounted on the side wall. The feed pusher slides prismatically along the chute axis, the lid rotates on a rear hinge, the drip drawer slides prismatically out from the lower front, the two rocker switches rotate on short pivots, and the basket rotates continuously about the vertical axis.
