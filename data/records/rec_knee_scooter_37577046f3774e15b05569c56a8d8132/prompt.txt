A compact indoor knee scooter with twin front swivel casters and narrow rear wheels. The low frame supports a central knee pad and a fixed handle post, with the rear wheel pair on a rigid axle and the front support on separate caster stems. The rear wheels spin on their axle supports, and each front caster wheel spins on its axle while swiveling on a vertical caster stem. Keep each front caster stem clipped into the frame mount so the swivel casters stay attached while turning.
