A padded windshield visor with a roof hinge bracket, a side retaining clip, and a vanity-mirror cover in the panel face, plus a small ticket clip along the inner edge. The mirror cover is a real inset flap rather than a printed rectangle, and the ticket clip remains a separate part mounted on the visor edge. The visor rotates on its main roof hinge, swivels at the secondary pivot near the bracket, and the mirror cover rotates on a small hinge in the visor face. Vehicle cabin scale.
