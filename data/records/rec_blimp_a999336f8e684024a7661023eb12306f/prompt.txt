A utility blimp with a thick envelope, a compact gondola, twin engine pods, and a boxy tail cruciform, plus a rear mooring wheel under the gondola tail. the gondola hangs from visible support struts below the hull centerline, and the wheel sits in a short support fork under the cabin. The propellers rotate continuously on their pod shafts, the rudder rotates on a vertical hinge line in the rear fin, and the elevators rotate on horizontal hinges in the tailplanes, and the mooring wheel rotates continuously on its small axle. Workhorse airship scale.
