A handheld clamp meter with a C-shaped measuring jaw at the top, a pistol-grip body, a central rotary selector, a rear tilt stand, and three front buttons below the display. The upper jaw rotates open on a hinge at the top of the head, the selector dial rotates continuously about its center axis, the rear stand folds out on a lower horizontal hinge, and each front button is a separate small prismatic push control. Handheld electrician-tool scale.
