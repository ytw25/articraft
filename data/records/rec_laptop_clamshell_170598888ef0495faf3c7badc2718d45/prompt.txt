A business ultrabook in a thin clamshell form with a webcam privacy shutter. The flat lower chassis carries the keyboard deck and centered touchpad, while the slim display lid is attached by two rear hinges and the small shutter sits in the top bezel. The lid rotates on the rear hinge axis, the keycaps move as short vertical prismatic plungers, and the privacy shutter translates sideways in its short guide track. Keep the webcam privacy shutter clipped in its short bezel track so it slides without leaving the top frame.
