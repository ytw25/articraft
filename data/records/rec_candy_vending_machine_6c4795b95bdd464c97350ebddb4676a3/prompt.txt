An octagonal jellybean dispenser with a faceted clear canister, a compact cast base, and a short front chute below the coin mechanism. The canister sits on the base with a distinct shoulder ring and a small service door breaks the back of the base. The front dispense knob rotates continuously on its shaft, the back service door rotates on a vertical side hinge, and the top refill cap rotates on a rear hinge. Countertop sweet-vendor scale.
