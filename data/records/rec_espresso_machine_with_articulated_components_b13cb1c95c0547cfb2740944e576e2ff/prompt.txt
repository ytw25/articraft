An espresso machine with an integrated grinder, a front group head, a steam wand, and a hinged bean-hopper lid above the grinder section. A removable drip tray sits below the brew area. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical side-support pivot, the hopper lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal lower-body guide.
