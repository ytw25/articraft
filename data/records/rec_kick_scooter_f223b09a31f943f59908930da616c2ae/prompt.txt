A stunt kick scooter with a short rigid deck, a one-piece handlebar, a slim fork, and a rear brake flap over the wheel. The rigid stem stays unfused from the deck so the steering joint remains readable. The front fork and handlebar yaw together on the steering axis, the front and rear wheels rotate continuously on their axles, and the rear brake flap rotates on its hinge over the back wheel. Park-scooter scale.
