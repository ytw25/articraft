A tabletop hibachi grill with a rectangular lower body and a domed cover. The shallow firebox stands on short feet, and the lid spans the full top opening with a rear hinge and a small front latch tab. The lid rotates upward on a horizontal rear hinge axis, and the latch tab rotates on its own small pivot at the front edge. Keep the domed cover clipped to the rear hinge line so the lid remains captured above the firebox during swing.
