A sliding compound miter saw assembly with twin horizontal rails. The base carries a turntable and fence, while the saw head rides on a carriage that slides fore-aft on the rails and also pivots downward from the rail carriage. The saw head pitches down on a transverse revolute axis through about 50 degrees, the carriage slides prismatically along the rail axis about 200 mm, and the turntable yaws on a vertical revolute axis about +/-45 degrees.
