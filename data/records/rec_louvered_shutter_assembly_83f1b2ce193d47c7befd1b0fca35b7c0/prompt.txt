An exterior storm shutter panel with wider slats and a stout outer frame. The single leaf is hinged to one side of the opening, and a vertical linkage bar near the free edge couples the adjustable louvers. The leaf rotates on a vertical side hinge axis, the louvers rotate on their horizontal end pivots, and the linkage bar translates vertically to move the slats together. Keep the wider louver slats clipped into the outer frame pivots so they stay seated during tilt motion.
