A recessed wall safe with a box-shaped body, thick front frame, heavy door, and circular combination dial on the door face. The safe body is fixed in the wall opening, the door is mounted on one side with exposed hinge support, and the dial sits on the outer door panel above or beside a simple handle. Joints: the door rotates on a revolute joint about its vertical hinge axis, the combination dial rotates continuously about its central outward-facing axis, and the handle rotates on a revolute joint about its own spindle axis.
