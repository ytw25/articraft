A long-shackle keyed padlock with a rectangular laminated body and a small dust cover over the keyway. One shackle leg is captive in the body as the pivot side, and the dust cover is hinged at the bottom of the key cylinder opening. The shackle rotates upward on the captive leg axis, and the dust cover rotates on its own small hinge pivot over the keyway. Keep the U-shaped shackle clipped into the body at its captive pivot leg so it stays attached when opened.
