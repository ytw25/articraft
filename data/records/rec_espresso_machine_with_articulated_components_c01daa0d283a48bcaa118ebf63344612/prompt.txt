A narrow apartment espresso machine with a compact group head, a short steam wand, a top water-tank lid, and a fold-down cup shelf on the front. A selector knob is mounted low on the side of the body. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical side pivot, the tank lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, the cup shelf rotates on a transverse lower-front hinge, and the selector knob rotates about its own short side-facing shaft axis.
