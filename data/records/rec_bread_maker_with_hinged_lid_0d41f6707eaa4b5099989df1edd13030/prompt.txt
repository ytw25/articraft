A compact bread maker with a boxy body, a rear-hinged lid, a right-side control panel with a timer knob and two square buttons, and a front lid-release plunger. The lid rotates upward on a rear revolute hinge, the timer knob rotates continuously, both square buttons are independent prismatic push buttons, the release plunger slides prismatically, and the internal kneading paddle rotates continuously on the base spindle within the pan opening. The baking chamber should remain a true hollow cavity sized for a loaf pan rather than a solid upper block. Small kitchen-appliance scale.
