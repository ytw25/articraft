A professional kitchen range with six burners, a deep front rail, and a lower oven door spanning most of the body width. Four rotary knobs occupy the center of the control rail, with one shorter knob on each far end and two push-buttons tucked directly under the middle pair. The six knobs rotate continuously about front-to-back revolute axes, the buttons are short prismatic plungers, and the oven door rotates downward on a bottom-edge hinge axis.
