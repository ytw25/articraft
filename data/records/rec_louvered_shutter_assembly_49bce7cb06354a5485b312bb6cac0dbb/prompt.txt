A cafe-style shutter set with two half-height louvered leaves. The paired leaves are framed panels hinged at the outer sides of the opening, and each panel has a small central knob on its tilt rod. The two leaves rotate on vertical side hinges, each louver rotates on its horizontal end pivots, the two tilt rods translate vertically, and the small knobs rotate on their local mounting axes. Keep the half-height louvers clipped into their side-stile pivots so the slats remain captured during adjustment.
