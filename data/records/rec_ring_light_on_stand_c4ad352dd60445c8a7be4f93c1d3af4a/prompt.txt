A selfie ring light on a light tripod with a center phone clamp. The ring head is carried by a small tilt yoke at the top of a telescoping center mast, and the mast stands on three fold-out tripod legs. The ring head rotates on a horizontal tilt axis through the yoke, the mast slides prismatically for height adjustment, the three tripod legs rotate on their hinge pivots at the hub, and the phone clamp jaws slide prismatically on their short guides. Keep the phone-clamp jaws clipped in their short guide tracks so the clamp stays captured while opening and closing.
