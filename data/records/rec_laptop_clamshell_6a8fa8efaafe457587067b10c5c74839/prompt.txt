A rugged field laptop with reinforced corners and front latch hooks. The lower body is a thick rectangular tub, the display lid is attached along the rear edge by exposed hinges, and two latch hooks at the front secure the lid closed. The lid rotates on the rear hinge axis, the keycaps move as short vertical prismatic plungers, and the two front latch hooks rotate on their small pivots at the front edge. Keep both front latch hooks clipped to their pivots at the lower front edge so they stay attached when swung open.
