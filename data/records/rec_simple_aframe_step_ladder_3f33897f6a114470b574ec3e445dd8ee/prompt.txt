Model Simple aframe step ladder as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from an A-frame ladder with paired legs, treads, and spread-limit braces, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Spread braces and leg pivots should define stable open/closed states. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
