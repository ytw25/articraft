A mini-ITX motherboard with two DIMM slots and a compact M.2 retention lever. The square board packs the CPU socket, rear I O shield area, and one main expansion slot around a small chipset heatsink. The two DIMM slot latches rotate on their hinge pivots, the PCIe slot latch rotates on its small pivot at the slot end, and the M.2 retention lever rotates on its tiny mounting axis near the card position. Keep the compact M.2 retention lever clipped to its tiny mounting pivot so it stays attached while rotating.
