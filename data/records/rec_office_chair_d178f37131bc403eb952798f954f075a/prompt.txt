An armless task chair with a five-star base, a gas column, and a tilting backrest. The wheeled base supports the seat on the center column, and the backrest is mounted to the rear of the seat mechanism. The caster wheels spin on their axles, the caster forks swivel on vertical pivots, the seat swivels about the center column, the gas column slides vertically for height adjustment, and the backrest rotates on a horizontal recline pivot behind the seat. Keep the central column clipped into both the seat support and the five-star base so the seat stays carried by the pedestal during swivel and height motion.
