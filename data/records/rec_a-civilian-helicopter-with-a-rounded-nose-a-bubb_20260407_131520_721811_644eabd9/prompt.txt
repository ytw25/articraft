A civilian helicopter with a rounded nose, a bubble-style cabin, a two-blade main rotor above the roof, and a tail boom with a small tail rotor mounted at the rear end. The fuselage has one hinged baggage door on the side behind the cabin, and the aircraft rests on skids under the body. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical rotor mast, the tail rotor rotates continuously about its transverse tail axis, and the baggage door rotates on a vertical side hinge on the fuselage.
