Design Tilting fan as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a base, guarded fan head, and side pivots for tilt control. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Fan tilt and rotor spin should be tied to explicit pivots and hubs. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
