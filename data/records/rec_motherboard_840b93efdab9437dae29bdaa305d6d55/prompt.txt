A server motherboard with narrow proportions, eight memory slots, and a small chipset blower. The rigid board carries a CPU socket with a hinged retention frame, several DIMM banks, and a compact round fan near the lower control area. The CPU retention frame rotates on its hinge axis, the retention lever rotates on its local pivot, the chipset blower rotates continuously about its local axis, and the DIMM latches rotate on their small hinges. Keep the CPU retention frame clipped to its hinge at the socket so the frame stays attached while opening.
