A long-reach desktop stapler with an elongated base, a tall rear hinge support, a long top arm, and a removable staple tray carried inside the upper rail. The top arm and upper rail rotate together on a rear horizontal revolute hinge, the staple tray slides prismatically for loading, the rear follower tab slides along the tray channel, and the front anvil plate rotates on a small pivot beneath the stapling nose. Mailroom or reception-desk scale.
