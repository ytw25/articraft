Model Turntable as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a plinth with rotating platter spindle and pivoting tonearm stage, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Platter and tonearm each need their own cleanly supported pivot systems. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
