A retro chrome espresso machine with a rounded body, a front group head, a side steam wand, and a hinged fill cap on the top deck. A small valve lever sits near the wand support. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the fill cap rotates on a transverse rear hinge, and the valve lever rotates on a short side-facing pivot axis.
