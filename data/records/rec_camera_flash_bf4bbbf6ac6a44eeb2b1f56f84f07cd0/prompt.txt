A professional camera flash with a tall rear control body, a broad mounting foot, and a forward-facing head between short yoke arms, plus a battery-door seam on one side of the body. the yoke makes the tilt axis visible on both sides of the head, and the battery door is carried as a distinct panel in the body shell. The head assembly swivels on a vertical revolute joint above the body and the head pitches on a horizontal revolute joint between the yoke arms, and the battery door rotates on a small side hinge.
