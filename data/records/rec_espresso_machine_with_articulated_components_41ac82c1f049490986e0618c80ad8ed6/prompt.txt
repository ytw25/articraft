A compact home espresso machine with a boxy body, a centered group head, a side steam wand, and a hinged lid over the rear water reservoir. A portafilter mounts under the group head and a shallow drip tray sits below the cup platform. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical side-support pivot, the reservoir lid rotates on a transverse rear hinge, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal base guide.
