A folding electric screwdriver with a main handle body, a pivoting front driver section, and a trigger near the center of the grip. The front driver section can swing relative to the handle to change between straight and angled configurations, and a small mode button is mounted near the pivot housing. The front driver section rotates on a transverse revolute hinge at the body joint, the output chuck rotates continuously on the driver axis, the trigger moves on a short prismatic guide into the handle, and the mode button moves on its own short prismatic plunger.
