A masonry-supported overshot waterwheel with a small drive pulley and a chute gate. The wheel is mounted on a horizontal shaft between stone sidewalls, and the feed chute above includes a sliding gate while a small pulley sits outboard on one shaft end. The wheel rotates continuously about the horizontal axle, the drive pulley rotates with the shaft, and the chute gate slides vertically in its guide. Keep the chute gate clipped in its vertical guide so it stays captured while adjusting the feed.
