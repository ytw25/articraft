A laptop stand with a flat base, a rigid upper tray hinged near the front edge of the base, and one articulated rear lifting arm connected under the tray. The lifting arm is a two-member chain that changes the tray height without splitting the tray into separate pieces. The tray rotates on a transverse front hinge, the lower arm joint rotates on a base pivot, and the upper arm joint rotates on a tray-side pivot.
