A folding support stay with a fixed root bracket, three flat bar links of different lengths, and a triangular end shoe. The links should overlap cleanly when folded and should read as stamped or fabricated hardware instead of generic placeholders. Give it realism with formed link edges, pressed ribs, shoulder bolts, end washers, and believable pad thickness at the grounded foot. The three link connections are revolute joints in one plane so the arm can fold near flat and extend outward. Keep every part mechanically grounded with no floating members, and make sure the articulation paths stay clear so nothing clips through covers, stops, housings, rails, or neighboring links.
