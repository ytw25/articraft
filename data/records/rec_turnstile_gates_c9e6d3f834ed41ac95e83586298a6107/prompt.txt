Model Turnstile gates as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a central post with radial arms rotating on a supported spindle, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Radial arm rotation should center on a supported spindle and bearing core. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
