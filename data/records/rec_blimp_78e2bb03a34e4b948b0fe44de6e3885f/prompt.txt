A rescue-service blimp with a long hull, a medium gondola, twin propulsion pods, and a reinforced cruciform tail, plus a rear mooring wheel under the gondola tail. the rear tail surfaces are thick enough to read as real aerodynamic panels instead of paper-thin fins, and the wheel sits in a short support fork under the cabin. The propellers rotate continuously on their pod shafts, the rudder panel rotates on a vertical tail hinge, and the elevator panels rotate on horizontal hinges at the horizontal fin roots, and the mooring wheel rotates continuously on its small axle. Emergency-service airship scale.
