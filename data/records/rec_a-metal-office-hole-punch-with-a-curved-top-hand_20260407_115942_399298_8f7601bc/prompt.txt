A metal office hole punch with a curved top handle, a rectangular base tray, and a rotating guide knob at one side of the paper stop. The punch body is fixed to the base, with the handle bridging over the punch head and a movable paper stop across the front. The handle rotates on a rear horizontal revolute hinge, the paper stop slides prismatically across the front guide, and the guide knob rotates on its own short shaft at the side of the stop.
