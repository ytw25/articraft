A two-finger manipulator core with a split palm block and two unequal finger chains mounted side by side on separate pedestals, one finger ending in a rounded pad and the other in a flat nib. Each finger uses three rigid phalanx links with exposed knuckle barrels and side plates instead of any soft organic form. Both fingers have their own base, middle, and distal revolute joints in one bending plane, and the two chains stay mechanically independent.
