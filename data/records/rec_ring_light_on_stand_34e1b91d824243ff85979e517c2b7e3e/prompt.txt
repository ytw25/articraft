A floor ring light with a two-section telescoping mast and a fold-out phone arm below the light head. The ring is mounted in a tilt yoke at the mast top, and the phone arm is hinged to the mast just beneath the ring opening. The ring head rotates on a horizontal tilt axis, the two mast sections slide prismatically for height adjustment, and the phone arm rotates on its hinge axis below the head. Keep the fold-out phone arm clipped to its hinge below the ring so it stays attached while rotating out.
