A wagon-style charcoal grill with a long barrel chamber and a front vent slider. The lower half of the barrel is fixed to a simple wheeled frame, the upper half forms the lid on a rear hinge line, and a shallow vent plate slides across slots near the front. The lid rotates on a rear revolute hinge axis, the two wagon wheels spin on their axle supports, and the vent plate translates prismatically across its guide slots. Keep the front vent plate clipped in its guide slots so it slides across the openings without separating from the barrel wall.
