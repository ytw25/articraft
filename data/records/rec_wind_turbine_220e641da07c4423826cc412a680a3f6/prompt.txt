Design Wind turbine as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a tower with nacelle, rotor hub, and blade-root interfaces. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Rotor and blade roots should be hub-mounted with realistic load paths. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
