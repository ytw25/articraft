A ring light on a boom stand with a yawing boom arm and a small counterweight. The vertical stand rises from a tripod base, the boom arm rotates from the stand top, and the ring light tilts at the boom tip on a compact yoke. The boom arm rotates about a vertical yaw axis at the stand top, the ring head rotates about a horizontal tilt axis at the boom tip, the center mast slides prismatically for height adjustment, and the tripod legs rotate on their hinge pivots at the base. Keep the boom arm clipped to its yaw joint at the stand top so the arm stays attached while rotating around the column.
