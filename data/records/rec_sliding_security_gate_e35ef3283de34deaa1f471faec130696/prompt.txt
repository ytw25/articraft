Design Sliding security gate as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a rigid gate leaf guided by track/roller hardware and latch geometry. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Gate travel should be constrained by rails/channels/rollers end-to-end. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
