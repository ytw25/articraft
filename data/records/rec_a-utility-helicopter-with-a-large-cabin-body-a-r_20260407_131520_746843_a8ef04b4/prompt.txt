A utility helicopter with a large cabin body, a roof-mounted main rotor hub, and a tail boom with a tail rotor mounted on one side of the rear fin. A side cargo door opens on the cabin and a rear engine service panel is mounted on the upper fuselage behind the mast. The main rotor rotates continuously about the vertical mast axis, the tail rotor rotates continuously about its lateral axis, the cargo door slides prismatically along the side of the cabin, and the service panel rotates on a side hinge at the rear upper fuselage.
