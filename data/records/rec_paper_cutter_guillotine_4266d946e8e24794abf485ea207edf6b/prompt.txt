A heavy paper cutter with a guillotine arm, a side gauge, and a fold-up extension table. The main cutting bed holds the blade pivot and fixed fence, the side gauge sets the cut width, and a support flap is hinged from one side of the base. The blade arm rotates on its corner pivot axis, the side gauge slides prismatically along its guide, and the extension table rotates on its side hinge axis. Keep the fold-up extension table clipped to its side hinge so the support flap stays attached while opening.
