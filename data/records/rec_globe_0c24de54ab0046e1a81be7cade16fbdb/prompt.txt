An illuminated classroom globe on a semicircular meridian with side tilt knobs. The sphere is supported between polar pivots inside the meridian, and the meridian is mounted to a pedestal yoke through two side trunnions with hand knobs. The globe rotates continuously about its polar axis, the meridian tilts on the horizontal trunnion axis, and the side knobs rotate on their small local axes. Keep the meridian ring clipped between the side trunnions of the pedestal yoke so the ring stays supported while tilting.
