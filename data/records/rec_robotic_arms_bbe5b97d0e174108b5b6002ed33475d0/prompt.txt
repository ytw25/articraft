Model Robotic arms as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a base pedestal feeding a shoulder-elbow-wrist chain with clear joint cartridges, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Make axis order and joint offsets readable so motion paths are unambiguous.
