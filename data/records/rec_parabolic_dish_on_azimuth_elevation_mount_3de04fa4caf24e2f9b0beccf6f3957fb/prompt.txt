A field tracking dish with a deep reflector, a rear yoke, and azimuth and elevation handwheels. The dish sits in side trunnions on a yoke above a rotating base, and one handwheel is mounted to the base while another is mounted to the elevation side plate. The base rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, the dish rotates about a horizontal elevation axis, and the two handwheels rotate on their local drive axes. Keep the deep reflector frame clipped between the elevation trunnions of the yoke so the dish stays supported while pitching.
