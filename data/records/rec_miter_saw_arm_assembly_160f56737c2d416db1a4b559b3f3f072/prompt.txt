A bench miter saw arm assembly with a turntable base and a guarded circular blade. The flat base supports a rotating table and fence, while a rear pivot yoke carries the swing arm, blade spindle, and lower blade guard. The saw head pitches downward on a transverse revolute axis at the rear yoke, the table yaws on a vertical revolute axis at the base center, the blade spins on its spindle axis, and the lower guard rotates on a small local hinge around the blade housing.
