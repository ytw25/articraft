Design Windshield wiper assembly as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a motor-crank linkage driving wiper pivots and blade carriers. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Crank-link sweep should be constrained by supported spindle pivots. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
