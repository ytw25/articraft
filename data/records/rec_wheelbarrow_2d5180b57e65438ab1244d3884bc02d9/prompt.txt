Design Wheelbarrow as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a tray, front wheel fork/axle, rear legs, and tubular handles. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Wheel/stance behavior should be obvious from axle, fork, and rear legs. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
