A salon ring light on a rolling U-base stand. The circular light head is mounted on a tilt bracket at the top of a vertical column, and the base carries four caster wheels under the U-shaped frame. The ring head rotates on a horizontal tilt axis, the stand column slides prismatically for height adjustment, and each caster wheel spins on its axle while swiveling on a vertical caster stem. Keep the ring head clipped into its tilt bracket at the top of the stand so the light stays supported while aiming.
