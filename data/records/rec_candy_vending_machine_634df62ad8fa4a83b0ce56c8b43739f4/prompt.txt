A wall-mounted quarter-candy dispenser with a rectangular clear hopper, a metal front plate, and a lower retrieval flap. The hopper hangs above the mechanism body on a flat backplate, with a small refill lid spanning the top edge. The front selector knob rotates continuously on its horizontal shaft, the retrieval flap rotates on a bottom hinge at the chute mouth, and the refill lid rotates on a rear hinge along the hopper top. Wall-mounted vending scale.
