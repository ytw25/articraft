Model Toaster oven as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a rectangular oven body with front door and shaft-driven control knobs, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Door hinges and control shafts should be obvious and manufacturable. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
