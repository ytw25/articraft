A lockable glove compartment with a narrow left-hinged door and a keyed cam latch. The fixed dashboard bin sits behind a rectangular opening, and the door mounts to one side wall on exposed hinges with the lock cylinder near the free edge. The door rotates on a vertical side hinge axis, the key cylinder rotates on its local axis, and the internal cam rotates with the lock at the latching edge. Keep the side-hinged door clipped into the exposed side hinges so it does not separate from the dashboard bin during swing.
