Model Searchlight tower as an industrial safety-first variant for heavy duty cycles. Start from a mast/tower support carrying a spotlight head in a pan-tilt yoke, then add guards, lockouts, over-travel stops, and reinforcement at high-stress joints. Favor visible fastener logic and plate/brace load paths over decorative surfacing. Pan and tilt axes should be separated and carried by yoke/bearing blocks. All subparts must remain connected to a parent support; no unsupported fragments. No floating parts.
