A compact wall air conditioner with a rounded front shell, a small control knob at one corner, and a lower outlet with directional vanes. The main housing is fixed to the wall plate, and the outlet has one broad flap in front of several narrow vertical guide vanes. The control knob rotates on its own short shaft, the broad outlet flap rotates on a horizontal hinge at the outlet edge, and the guide vanes rotate independently on vertical pivots inside the outlet.
