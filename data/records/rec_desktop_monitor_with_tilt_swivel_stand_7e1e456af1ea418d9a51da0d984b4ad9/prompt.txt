A 24-inch office desktop monitor with a rectangular display shell, a slim central neck, and a flat pedestal base, plus a power rocker and three menu buttons at the underside of the bezel. the lower bezel leaves room for visible front-panel controls instead of a blank strip, and the rocker and buttons stay distinct from one another. The screen housing tilts on a horizontal revolute hinge at the top of the neck and the stand swivels continuously about a vertical axis above the base, and the power rocker rotates on a short local pivot and the menu buttons depress independently. Standard office-desk scale.
