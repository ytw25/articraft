A zero-gravity style lounge chair with a suspended seat sling, a separate reclining back panel, and an elevating leg rest. The side frame carries the back panel on upper pivots and the leg rest on lower side links ahead of the seat. The backrest rotates on a horizontal side pivot axis, and the footrest rotates independently on its own front-side hinge axis through the support links. Keep the elevating leg rest clipped to its side support links so the rest remains attached through its rotation.
