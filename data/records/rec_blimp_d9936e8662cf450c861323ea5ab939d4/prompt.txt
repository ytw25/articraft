A communications blimp with a cigar envelope, a rectangular equipment gondola, side engine pods, and a four-fin tail, plus a rear mooring wheel under the gondola tail. the equipment gondola remains a distinct suspended body under the hull centerline, and the wheel sits in a short support fork under the cabin. The two propellers spin continuously on their local shaft axes, the vertical tail rudder rotates on a hinge line at the fin trailing edge, and the horizontal control surfaces rotate on horizontal tail hinges, and the mooring wheel rotates continuously on its small axle. Utility communications airship scale.
