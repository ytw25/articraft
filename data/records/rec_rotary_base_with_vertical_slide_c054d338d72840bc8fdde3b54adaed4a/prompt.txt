A pedestal indexer with a round turntable base, a rigid upper mast mounted on the turntable, and a twin-rail vertical carriage on the mast. The base and mast should read as one rotating lower assembly carrying a clearly separate lift stage above it. Make it feel like real positioning hardware with machined turntable faces, guide rails, carriage caps, stiffened mast walls, and believable stop features. The pedestal rotates on a vertical revolute axis and the upper carriage moves on a vertical prismatic axis. Keep every part mechanically grounded with no floating members, and make sure the articulation paths stay clear so nothing clips through covers, stops, housings, rails, or neighboring links.
