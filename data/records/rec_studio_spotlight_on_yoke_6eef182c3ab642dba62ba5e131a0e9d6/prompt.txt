Design Studio spotlight on yoke as a field-service workhorse. Build it around a spotlight can captured by side yokes over a stable stand/base. Prioritize chunky hardware, replaceable wear parts, clear maintenance access, and robust tolerances for repeated use. Tilt aiming must be carried by side yokes with lockable friction points. Every part must be connected by real brackets, shafts, hinges, rails, collars, weldments, or housings; absolutely no floating geometry. No floating parts.
