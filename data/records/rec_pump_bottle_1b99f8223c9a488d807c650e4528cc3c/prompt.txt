A salon pump bottle with a long side spout and a tall pump head. The fixed body carries the pump collar and plunger stem, and the long spout head is mounted so it can rotate slightly to point left or right. The pump head slides vertically on a short prismatic stroke, and the spout head rotates about the vertical stem axis at the top of the bottle. Keep the long side spout head clipped on the vertical stem so it stays attached while rotating left and right.
