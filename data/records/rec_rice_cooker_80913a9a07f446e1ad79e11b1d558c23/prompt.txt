A family-size rice cooker with a wide rounded shell, a rear lid hinge band, and a compact control cluster on the lower front face, plus a folding carry handle across the upper shoulders and a power button below the display. The shell stays low and broad instead of reading like a pressure cooker, and the handle stays clearly separated from the main shell surfaces. Kitchen counter scale.
