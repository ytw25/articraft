A benchtop teaching microscope with a broad base, a monocular tube, an objective turret, and a mechanical stage with a side carriage, plus a diaphragm lever under the stage. the mechanical stage remains clearly separate from the supporting base and arm, and the lever is supported by the condenser housing instead of floating under the stage. The objective turret rotates continuously on the nosepiece axis, the optical body slides prismatically on the focusing column, and the side carriage slides prismatically along the stage guide, and the diaphragm lever rotates on a short pivot beneath the stage. Teaching-lab scale.
