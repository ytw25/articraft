An Alice-style ergonomic keyboard with a split angled main cluster in one continuous case. The housing has a central notch, two inward-canted key fields, and a pair of folding rear tent feet under the back corners. The keys move as short vertical prismatic plungers, and the two tent feet rotate on small hinge axes to change the typing angle. Keep the rear tent feet clipped to their hinge mounts under the back corners so the ergonomic case stays supported during adjustment.
