A double-panel shutter assembly with two framed leaves meeting at the center of a window opening. Each leaf is hinged to an outer jamb and contains its own stack of adjustable horizontal louvers connected by a tilt rod. The two leaves rotate on vertical hinge axes at the outer jambs, each louver rotates on its horizontal side pivots, and each tilt rod translates vertically to coordinate the louver angles. Keep each leaf's louver stack clipped into its side-stile pivots so the slats stay captured as the rods drive them.
