A gaming monitor with a broad screen, a V-shaped base, and a stand that supports height, swivel, and portrait rotation. The display mounts to a rear stand head above the base, and a power button sits at the lower front edge. The center column slides prismatically for height adjustment, the stand swivels on a vertical axis at the base joint, the screen rotates on a horizontal tilt hinge at the stand head, the screen rotates on a portrait pivot behind the panel, and the power button moves on a short prismatic guide.
