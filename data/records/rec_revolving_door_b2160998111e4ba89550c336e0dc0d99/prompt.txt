A two-wing revolving door with accordion-fold two-leaf bypass openings on each side. The drum carries two diametrically opposite wings on a central shaft; each bypass opening is closed by two hinged leaves — an outer leaf hinged to the frame post and an inner leaf at the outer-leaf edge — that fold in sequence. The drum rotates continuously; each outer bypass leaf pivots on a revolute at the frame post; each inner bypass leaf pivots on a revolute at the outer-to-inner knuckle.
