A home espresso machine with a rounded body, a front group head, a side steam wand, and a top fill flap over the water chamber. A side power rocker and a small lower drip tray sit on the body shell. The portafilter rotates into the group head about the vertical brew axis, the steam wand rotates on a vertical support pivot, the fill flap rotates on a transverse rear hinge, the power rocker rotates on a short side-facing pivot axis, and the drip tray slides prismatically forward along a longitudinal lower-front guide.
