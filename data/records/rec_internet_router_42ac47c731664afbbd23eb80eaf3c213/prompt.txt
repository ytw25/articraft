A rack-style small-office router with two swing-up antennas and a front status door. The low rectangular chassis has a narrow hinged cover over the indicator strip, and two stub antennas are mounted near the back corners. The two antennas rotate on their small hinge bases, and the front status cover rotates on a horizontal hinge axis along its lower edge. Keep the front status door clipped to its lower hinge line so the narrow cover stays attached while opening.
