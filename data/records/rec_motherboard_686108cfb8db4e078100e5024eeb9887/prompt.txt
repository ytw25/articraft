A micro-ATX motherboard with four memory slots and a reinforced graphics slot. The board layout places the CPU socket near the upper center, the expansion area along the lower half, and a few hinged latches around the socket and memory banks. The DIMM slot latches rotate on their small hinges, the reinforced graphics-slot latch rotates on its end pivot, and the CPU socket lever rotates on its own local axis. Keep the reinforced graphics-slot latch clipped to its slot-end pivot so the latch arm stays attached during release motion.
