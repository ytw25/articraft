A sightseeing blimp with a streamlined main envelope, a long passenger cabin, and one propeller pod mounted on each side near the cabin, plus a rear mooring wheel under the gondola tail. the tail keeps a real cruciform fin arrangement at the rear of the hull, and the wheel sits in a short support fork under the cabin. The two propellers spin continuously on their shaft axes, the rudder pivots on a vertical tail hinge, and the elevators pivot on horizontal hinges at the tailplane roots, and the mooring wheel rotates continuously on its small axle. Passenger airship scale.
