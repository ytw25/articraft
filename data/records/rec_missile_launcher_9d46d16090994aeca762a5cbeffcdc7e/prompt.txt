A twin-rail missile launcher on a trainable pedestal. The fixed pedestal supports a yaw turntable, and an elevation cradle above it carries two parallel launch rails with a small sighting box at one side. The pedestal rotates about a vertical azimuth axis, and the rail cradle rotates about a horizontal elevation axis between its side supports. Keep the twin-rail cradle clipped between the side trunnions of the yaw head so it stays captured while elevating.
