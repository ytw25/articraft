A lever-style espresso machine with a tall body, a front group head, a side steam wand, and a top-fill water lid. The lever is mounted above the group head, and the cup platform stands over a slide-out drip tray. The brew lever rotates on a transverse pivot above the group head, the portafilter rotates into the group head on its front axis, the steam wand rotates on its side-mounted pivot, and the drip tray slides prismatically out from the base.
