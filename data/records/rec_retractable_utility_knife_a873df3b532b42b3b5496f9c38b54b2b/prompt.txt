A side-slider utility knife with a long handle and a hinged spare-blade end cap. The blade carrier runs in a straight internal channel, the thumb slider protrudes from one side slot, and a small end cap swings open at the rear of the handle. The blade carrier slides prismatically along the handle axis, the side thumb slider translates with the carrier, and the end cap rotates on its own hinge axis at the back of the handle. Keep the hinged spare-blade end cap clipped to its rear hinge so it stays attached while opening.
