A flat-incline utility bench with a rectangular base, a hinged backrest, a smaller seat pad, and a rear ladder support, plus a foot-roller assembly at the front end. the support ladder sits behind the backrest bracket rather than disappearing inside the frame, and the roller pair is mounted on a rigid front yoke. The backrest rotates upward on a horizontal hinge at the seat junction, the seat pivots on a separate front hinge, and the rear ladder support rotates on a lower pivot at the back of the frame, and the two front rollers spin continuously on their shared support axle. Garage-gym bench scale.
