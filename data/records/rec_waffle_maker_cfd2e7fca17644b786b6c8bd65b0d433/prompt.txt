A flip-style Belgian waffle maker with a circular cooking shell carried in a side stand frame, plus a thermostat dial on the side wall. the cooking shell and stand remain clearly separate supported assemblies, and the dial remains a separate control on the housing side. The cooking shell opens on its own rear lid hinge and the whole shell rotates continuously on the horizontal flip axis between the side supports, and the thermostat dial rotates continuously on its short shaft. Breakfast-station appliance scale.
