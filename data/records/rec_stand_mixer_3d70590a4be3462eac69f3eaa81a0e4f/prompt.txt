A commercial bakery stand mixer scaled for a worktable, with a heavy motor pedestal, a thick rolled-rim bowl on a short carriage, and a robust tilt head carrying a spiral dough hook. The bowl adjusts on a short prismatic slide at the base front, the head opens upward on a rear horizontal revolute hinge, the attachment rotates continuously about the vertical spindle axis, the speed selector on the base rotates on a revolute pivot, and the lock release on the base slides prismatically.
