A sliding compound miter saw assembly with twin horizontal rails. The base carries a turntable and fence, the saw head rides on a carriage along the rails, and the blade sits inside a guarded motor housing. The saw head pitches on a transverse revolute axis at the carriage, the carriage slides prismatically along the rail axis, the turntable yaws on a vertical revolute axis at the base, and the blade spins about its spindle axis.
