A portable mixer case with a deep lower shell and a broad lid supported by short stay links. The body is a rounded rectangle with a rear piano hinge, and a pair of internal lid stays connect the lid to the lower shell walls. The lid rotates on the rear horizontal hinge axis, and the two stay links rotate on their support pivots between the shell and the lid. Keep the broad lid clipped to the rear hinge and the short stay-link pivots so it remains supported as it opens.
