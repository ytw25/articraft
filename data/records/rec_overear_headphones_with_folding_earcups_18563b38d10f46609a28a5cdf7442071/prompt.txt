A studio over-ear headphone set with large round cups, sliding headband extenders, and compact fold hinges. The earcups sit in short yokes at the ends of the headband arms, and each arm has a telescoping size adjuster above the fold hinge. Each cup swivels on its yoke pivot, each fold hinge rotates to collapse the headphone, and the headband extenders slide prismatically within the arm sleeves. Keep the telescoping headband extenders clipped inside the arm sleeves so the sliders stay captured during size adjustment.
