A gaming desktop monitor with a wide curved screen shell, a split rear support yoke, and a tripod-style base with a control cluster on the underside of the lower edge. The display tilts on a horizontal revolute joint carried between the yoke arms, the monitor swivels continuously about a vertical axis at the base of the stand, and the visible underside control buttons are independent small prismatic push buttons rather than one fused strip. Large desk gaming setup scale.
