A creator-focused desktop monitor with a flat display shell, a stout center column, and a rectangular base plate, plus a small bank of menu buttons under the lower bezel. the stand head keeps a real tilt-and-pivot carriage behind the panel instead of a thin cosmetic neck, and the controls are separate parts instead of one fused panel. The display tilts on a horizontal hinge at the stand head, rotates continuously about its center axis for portrait mode, and the center column slides prismatically for height adjustment, and the menu buttons articulate independently as small prismatic push controls.
