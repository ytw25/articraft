A compact laboratory monocular microscope with a heavy base, a swept arm, a monocular head, and a rectangular specimen stage, plus a diaphragm lever under the stage. the stage and optical body stay as clearly separate supported assemblies, and the lever is supported by the condenser housing instead of floating under the stage. The optical body carriage slides prismatically on the vertical column, the objective turret rotates continuously about the optical axis, and the stage carriage slides prismatically left to right, and the diaphragm lever rotates on a short pivot beneath the stage. Benchtop lab scale.
