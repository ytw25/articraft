A 24-inch office desktop monitor with a rectangular display shell, a slim central neck, and a flat pedestal base, plus a small bank of menu buttons under the lower bezel. the lower bezel leaves room for visible front-panel controls instead of a blank strip, and the controls are separate parts instead of one fused panel. The screen housing tilts on a horizontal revolute hinge at the top of the neck and the stand swivels continuously about a vertical axis above the base, and the menu buttons articulate independently as small prismatic push controls. Standard office-desk scale.
