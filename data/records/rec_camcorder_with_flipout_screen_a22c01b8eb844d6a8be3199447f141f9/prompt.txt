A compact HD camcorder with a rounded right-hand grip, a short lens housing, and a side LCD monitor, plus three small front-side buttons below the screen opening. the monitor is mounted on a clear hinge spine at the left side of the body, and the buttons are separate controls in the body wall. The LCD monitor rotates outward on a vertical hinge line and the lens ring rotates continuously around the front barrel, and the three buttons each depress independently as small prismatic push controls. Consumer camera scale.
