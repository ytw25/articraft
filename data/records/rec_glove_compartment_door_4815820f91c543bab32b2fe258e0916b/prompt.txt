A rugged off-road glove compartment with a side-hinged panel and a large quarter-turn latch. The bin is fixed in a thick dashboard frame, and the door is attached to one jamb by two exposed hinges with the latch at the opposite side. The door swings on a vertical revolute hinge axis, and the quarter-turn latch rotates on its own axis in the center of the door face. Keep the door panel clipped into the exposed side hinges so the quarter-turn latch does not end up carrying the panel by itself.
